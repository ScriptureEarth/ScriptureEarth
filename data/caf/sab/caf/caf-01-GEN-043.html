Joseph boonokah 'en Benjamin bulh whusanahedel
43
K'an ndi dai cho whuzdli 'et yun k'ut tube 'uhooja.
2 'Et hukw'elh'az, haniyeh Egypt whuz hahiyanla, 'i ts'iya hiyanyi hoh hube'abá 'en 'obulhni, “K'ah nahdulh 'ink'ez ts'uyiyaz neba ohket.”
3 'Et whunts'i Judah 'en 'uyulhni, “Dune ts'ih'un cho hoh neghu dani 'et njan ndutni, ‘'Aw sunen 'i noolh'en ghait'ah nuhwhuchistl'ah nuhwhulh 'int'ah k'an zeh.’
4 Nechistl'ah 'en nuwhulhtalh'alh t'eh, 'et sih ntsun uztoodelh 'ink'ez ts'uyi mba ts'oket.
5 'Et whunts'i 'aw nuwhulh tolh'a ghait'ah t'eh, 'aw ntsun chatuzadudulh, dune 'en njan ndun whulhni, ‘'Aw sunen 'i noolh'en ghait'ah nuhchistl'ah nuhwhulh 'int'ah k'an zeh’.”
6 Israel 'en 'et 'utni, “Dant'i ka stabe 'uts'un such'a 'uwhahla hoh 'ahja, dune 'en ndudahni, nk'a chah nuhwhe'ilhunah dune hooni dahni?”
7 'Ink'ez 'et 'uhutni, “Dune 'en soo ts'ih'un cho neoodulhkut, nuhwheni 'ink'ez nelh hedoh, njan ndutni, ‘Nuhwhe'abá nk'a chah ih khuna? Nuhwhe'ilhunah 'en 'uyoon cha ih hooni?’ 'Et nts'e na'a datni, 'et ndudidutni. Nts'e na'a simba nt'eoonodudzin njan ndutni 'et, ‘Nuhwhuchistl'ah dez toolhte’?”
8 'Et Judah 'en be'abá Israel 'uyulhni, “Nyaz sulh tolh'a, 'aw 'et duk'iduja 'ink'ez todudelh, 'et sih tsukhoona 'ink'ez yachatsosdla, 'anwhunelht'et nuwheni, nyun, 'ink'ez nuwhuskehyaz chah.
9 Si, sih bughosdle. Sula k'ez ts'un 'onleh. 'Aw nts'un hanachalhtel, 'ink'ez mbut ninachalhtel t'eh, 'et sih nts'e whuts'un sk'eholts'it.
10 'Et njan nduchaidut'ah inle t'eh, 'uda da whulh nat whusunaidudel inle.”
11 'Et hube'abá Israel 'en 'obulhni, “'Et ndutehnilh t'eh, ndi 'i bugha yóohlelh. Njan yun k'ut soo deghunla unzoo mai, 'i ts'ai be dune buts'un toohka, bulahyaz 'i balm, honey, yoo sooltsun, myrrh, pistachio nuts, 'inkez almond chah.
12 Nla k'ez sooneya nawh 'uneltsuk 'i natooh'a, 'ink'ez nuhla k'ez ndi sooneya nawhe'ezdlai budah yunez'ai, 'i chah k'ah nantooh'a, 'aw chahiyí'il sih 'int'ah.
13 Nuhwhuchistl'ah cha 'ooneh, 'ink'ez didahdel 'ink'ez dune ts'un natoohdelh.
14 Yak'usda Degha Nus 'Ultus (El Shaddai), 'en sih ye negha te'ninzun nuhgha ho'alh dune bubut 'et nuhwhe'ilhunah 'ilhunah 'ink'ez Benjamin 'en nuhts'un hubula nadootnih sih. 'Et 'uskeh cha sust'ih suli, 'en k'e tube cho sudzi ndesda t'eh, tube cho hoh sudzi ndesda teleh.”
15 Hoh dunekah ndi yugha ninla 'i hitezdla, 'ink'ez Benjamin chah hitelhti, 'ink'ez sooneya nat hubula k'ez, dihudindel 'ink'ez njan Egypt ts'un whehandel. 'Ink'ez Joseph hibut nudelhuya.
16 Joseph 'en Benjamin hubulh 'int'ah ho'en, 'aw 'et bukoo ba huwa 'a'aih ba ne'duni, 'en 'uyulhni, “Ndunnah dune 'en sukooz ts'i hubanlelh 'ink'ez khunai nasolhghelh, 'ink'ez lhadonle. Ndunnah dune 'en sulh 'uhuteyi soo ts'etniz 'et.”
17 Dune 'en soo nts'e na'a Joseph datni, 'et soo nduja 'ink'ez dune 'en ndunnah dunekah Joseph bukoo 'et dabuninla.
18 K'an 'et dunekah whehunelhujoot Joseph bukoo dabaninla 'et huwa. 'Ink'ez 'et 'uhutni, “Ndi sooneya 'i 'udechoo ne'ezdlai be k'ah ninahine'ai 'et huwa. Njan dan whuts'uninla, 'et huwa sih nek'ehote'alh 'ink'ez nehutelhghel, nedonkey bulh 'ulhna nehutelhtsil.”
19 Hoh Joseph bukoo ba hawa a'aih ba ne'duni higha ninindel hoh 'et koo whuda'dentan k'ah 'et hiyulh yalhtuk.
20 'Et 'uhiyulhni, “O nemoodih njan ndiduja, soo 'udechoo ts'uyi ootadukulh wheni whusaidudel da,
21 'et nduhooja, 'et ninantadutus hoh nuwhe'ezdlai 'i budah lhch'a 'uhoodulya hoh 'ilhunah dune totsuk hubuzooneya 'i bubu'ezdlai budah, yunez'ai nezooneya ts'iyawh 'et 'int'ah hoh nuwhula k'ez be k'ah hananadudzai.
22 'Ink'ez sooneya 'uyoo chah nela k'ez hanadut'ai ts'uyi be ootadukulh wheni. 'Aw nt'echawhunidudzun ndan simba 'uja nezooneya 'i nuwhe'ezdlai be naina'ai 'et.”
23 'Et 'utni, “Nuhwhulh whudooghel. 'Aw whechanuzulhujut. NuhYak'usda (Elohim) 'en nuhwhe'abá buYak'usda (Elohim) 'en nuhgha ininla 'int'ah 'ezdlai bet 'et. Nuhzooneya 'i nunus'a.” 'Ink'ez Simeon hubuts'un tinainilhti.
24 Hoh ndunah dune 'en dunekah Joseph bukoo 'et dabininla 'ink'ez too hubagha nílhdze 'ink'ez hubuke toonahuzguz 'ink'ez hubudonkey 'i chah yugha'ni'ai.
25 'Et ndi Joseph higha itelelh, 'i lhahiduleh, Joseph dzetniz hateyalh wheni, 'et 'uhoozalhts'a lhes ha teyi 'et.
26 Joseph bukoo nanja hoh, ndi higha hininla 'i hubula k'ez hiyelelh hoh koo dahininla 'ink'ez hiba yuk 'uhuja yun ts'ih.
27 'Ink'ez 'et huboodulhkut dahint'ah 'et, 'ink'ez 'et 'utni, “Nuhwhe'abá 'en soo 'int'ah ih? Dune nus uzulh 'en? 'En bughi yalhtuk, nk'at chah ih khuna?”
28 'Et 'uhutni, “Nye'ulhna ne'abá 'en soo 'int'ah, nk'a chah khuna.” 'Ink'ez hubutsi be yuk 'uhuja 'ink'ez nahantez.
29 'Et buna be duk yuta'en hoh buchistl'ah Benjamin 'en yutelh'en, be'ama buyaz 'ink'ez 'utni, “Ndunah 'en ih nuhchistl'ah suts'un bughu yalhtuk 'en?” 'Ink'ez 'et 'utni, “Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en nts'un wuzoo, suye'.”
30 K'an hoh budzi tube cho hoh buchistl'ah yuk'eninli, hoh Joseph 'ats'ih hoh whe'iz ts'ih whenya 'ink'ez nadutse. Bukoo wheoodi'ai, 'et dananja 'ink'ez 'et intse.
31 'Et bunen ni'nalhde 'ink'ez 'az tinanja, 'ink'ez soo na'dudilya 'ink'ez 'utni, “Lhes huwanah'ai.”
32 Hoh dich'oh ba 'udooh hiba ni'ninla 'ink'ez hedidut chah hedich'oh 'udooh, 'ink'ez Egypt whut'en ndan hiyulh 'uyi, 'en chah hedich'oh, Egypt whut'en 'en 'aw Hebrew whut'en bulh 'uhooyi ba cha'hoot'ah. Egypt whut'en huba me hoonli.
33 'Et hubut natl'ahudelts'i, 'udetsah huhooni hukw'u na'a, 'ink'ez 'udek'ah k'an huneyelh hukw'u na'a, 'ink'ez dunekah huba hooncha hoh lhanul'en.
34 'Et hubunalh hubugha ni'ai, 'et whunts'i Benjamin 'en skwunlai 'o 'un uneltsuk 'i yugha ini'ai dunekah hubugha 'un 'unelts'uk. Hoh hiyulh tahetnai 'ink'ez honalhnik.
