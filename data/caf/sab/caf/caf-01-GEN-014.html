Lot ilhchoot 'ink'ez yu'ih nunazja
14
'Et hukw'elh'az inle hoh Amraphel 'en yun k'ut Shinar hubudayicho da, Arioch 'en yun k'ut Ellasar hubudayicho da, Chedorlaomer 'en yun k'ut Elam hubudayicho da, 'ink'ez Tidal 'en ndunnah lhelhts'un hedoh hubudayicho unli da.
2 'En ndunnah 'en huba huteghan ndunnah Bera, 'en keyah Sodom hubudayicho, Birsha 'en keyah Gomorrah hubudayicho, Shinab 'en keyah Admah hubudayicho, Shemeber 'en keyah Zeboiim, 'ink'ez keyah Bela hubudayicho (njan Belah, 'i keyah Zoar chah hiyulhni), 'en ts'iyawh lhts'udughan nihoni'ai.
3 'En ts'iyawh ulhts'un nihudendel njan Siddim whukoh 'et, 'et 'uhoontah yatoo lusel.
4 Lanezi 'o 'at nankoh (12) nawhudezut hoh ndunnah hubudayikah 'et ndun hubudayi Chedorlaomer 'en hinane'ust'en da 'ink'ez whulh lanezi 'o 'at tat (13) nawhudezut 'et hich'ats'un 'uja.
5 'Et hoh whulh lanezi 'o 'at dit (14) nawhudezut 'et Chedorlaomer 'ink'ez ndun hubudayicho hiyulh 'ut'en, 'en ndunnah Repha whut'en, 'en yun k'ut Ashteroth Karnaim whudune, ndunnah Zuz whut'en, 'en yun k'ut Ham whudune, 'ink'ez ndunnah Em whut'en, 'en yun k'ut Shaveh Kiriathaim whudune,
6 'ink'ez ndunnah Hor whut'en 'en hubudzulh Seir 'et El Paran whuts'un 'uhoolhdza, 'et njan tinta whegha 'uhoont'ah.
7 'Ink'ez 'et lht'anahunesdel 'ink'ez njan yun k'ut, En Mishpat, 'et njan yun k'ut, Kadesh cha hiyulhni, 'ink'ez ndunnah Amalek whut'en ndulhcho whuyun k'ut, 'ink'ez ndunnah Amor whut'en, ndan yun k'ut Hazezon Tamar huwhut'i, 'en chah, 'en ts'iyawh hubehunindel.
8 'Et Sodom hubudayicho, Gomorrah hubudayicho, Admah hubudayicho, Zeboiim hubudayicho, Bela hubudayicho, 'i Zoar chah hiyulhni, 'en ts'iyawh 'un whehandel 'ink'ez 'ilho lhtahunindel hoh lhahudughan, njan Siddim whukoh 'et.
9 'Et ndunnah hubuch'a 'uhint'ah, ndun Chedorlaomer 'en Elam whut'en hubudayicho, Tidal 'en Goiim hubudayicho, Amraphel 'en Shinar whut'en hubudayicho, 'ink'ez Arioch 'en Ellasar whut'en hubudayicho, 'en dihult'oh, 'ink'ez ndunnah dinah, 'en skwunlatnah ts'un hube hudulh.
10 'Ink'ez Siddim whukoh 'et tsak'et dzeh hoonli whulai. 'Et Sodom hubudayicho 'ink'ez Gomorrah hubudayicho 'en ts'ih huteldel hoh bulunah 'en njan dzeh tsak'et hankat, 'ink'ez bulunah 'en dzulh ts'un whets'ih halhdel.
11 'Ink'ez 'et Sodom 'ink'ez Gomorrah hube'ilhdzun 'i ts'iyawh hiyilhchoot 'ink'ez ndai hubuts'uyi 'i chah ts'iyawh hiyilhchoot hoh whunahedel.
12 Lot, 'en chah hiyilhchoot, Abram boono, 'en buye', Sodom 'et whut'i hoh be'ildzun 'i chah ts'iyawh 'ilhuhigezdla 'ink'ez whenahedel.
13 'Et 'ilhunah hubughutelhlha hoh Abram, Hebrew 'int'ah, 'en njan duchun terebinth, oak hiyulhni, 'et whut'i. Ndi duchun 'i ndun Amor whut'en, Mamre 'int'ah, 'en be'ildzun 'int'ah. Boono Eshcol 'ink'ez boono Aner 'en, Abram hits'un detni 'uhint'ah.
14 'Et hoh Abram 'en bulhutsin ts'ilhchoot whulh 'udants'o hoh, 'et 'aw 'et hiyulh 'ilhudughan 'en 318 dune huhoodul'e hubunane'hut'en, hubukoo yahuhonle 'en hubuhoolhdzih, yun k'ut Dan 'uhoolhdza whuts'un.
15 Hiyulh lhudughan 'en 'ulhk'ebanla 'ink'ez 'ulh'ek hoh hubuhoolhdel, hiyulh lhudughan 'en bulh nde keyah Hobah ts'un 'uhooldza 'et nebanenyoot. 'Et keyah Damascus ts'un huwunyaz de.
16 'Et hoh hube'ilhdzun 'i ts'iyawh hanayanla 'ink'ez bulhutsin Lot, 'en chah hanayalhti, hubunaih, ts'ekoo, 'ink'ez 'o 'un dune hinli chah.
17 'Et hukw'elh'az hubudayicho Chedorlaomer hubuch'a'nilhde da, 'ink'ez ndun hubudayicho hiyulh 'ut'en da chah, 'et Sodom hubudayicho 'en Shaveh whukoh 'et hubudezya. 'Et hubudayicho bukoh 'uhoont'ah.
18 'Ink'ez keyah Salem hubudayicho 'en Melchizedek, 'en lhes 'ink'ez mai too bulh 'i hayanla. 'En hoh Yak'usda Soo Deghanus 'Int'ah (El Elyon)* 'en ba nawhulnuk 'int'ah.
19 'Ink'ez hoonzoo 'obulhni 'ink'ez njan 'utni,
“Abram 'en hoonzoo ba 'uhot'e,
'en buYak'usda Soo Deghanus 'Int'ah (El Elyon),
'en 'int'ah yun k'ut 'ink'ez yaz 'uhoonla.
20 Yak'usda Soo Deghanus Dezti 'Int'ah (El Elyon),
'en hoonzoo 'obulhni, 'en ba 'uhot'e.
'En 'uja nch'ahudetni 'en nla
k'ebanla 'ink'ez 'en 'uja.”
'Ink'ez Abram 'en 'uja Melchizedek, 'en ts'iyaitsuk hoh lanezi whinghaz (10%) 'et yugha óoninla.
21 'Et Sodom hubudayicho 'en Abram ts'un 'utni, “Sudune 'en sgha buninle, 'ink'ez dant'i int'i, 'i nyun ch'oh 'olhdzin”.
22 'Et Abram 'en Sodom hubudayicho 'en 'uyulhni, “Moodihti (Yahweh), Yak'usda Soo Deghanus Dezti (El Elyon), 'en buts'un didulhlhunik, yun k'ut 'ink'ez yak'uz 'uhoonla 'en.
23 'Aw dant'i whunts'i whulhchoot ghait'ah. Yabats'eh 'ink'ez ke tl'oolh whunts'i 'ink'ez 'et 'aw dant'i nye'ildzun, 'i 'aw whulhchoot ghait'ah. 'Et ndusudini 'et k'an zeh sih, ‘Abram, 'en soode'a silhtsi’.
24 'Ink'ez 'et ndai chilhkah hiyanyi, 'i zeh 'ink'ez ndai dune hube'ilhdzun sulh hahandel, 'en Aner, Eshcol, 'ink'ez Mamre, 'en hube'ildzun 'i hitoola.”
