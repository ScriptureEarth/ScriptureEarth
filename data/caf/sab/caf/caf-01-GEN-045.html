Joseph bubulhutsinkah bunalh haho'ai
45
Joseph 'en 'aw 'udodutun ghainil, ndunnah ts'iyawh hibut nudelhuya 'et khunekti be yaihtuk, “Ts'iyannah sch'a 'un toodelh!” ni hoh 'aw 'ilhunnah whunts'i yulh chandilyah. Hoh Joseph ndan 'int'ah 'et bulhutsinkah 'en ndo bulhni.
2 Hoh tube cho 'utso hoh 'ink'ez Egypt whut'en 'ink'ez Pharaoh bukoo hudelhts'i 'et hiduzts'ai.
3 'Et Joseph bubulhtsinkah 'en ndo bulhni, “Si Joseph 'ust'ah. Se'abá nk'achah ih khuna?” 'Et whunts'i bubulhutsinkah 'aw whe' hidoni hooyal. Tube hubach'a chailel 'et huwa ndunnah hubulh 'int'ah 'et.
4 Joseph bubulhutsinkah 'en 'obulhni, “Sla ahneh, sugha ninahdelh.” Hoh higha ninindel, 'et 'utni, “Si 'ust'ah Joseph nuhwhuchistl'ah, 'en Egypt buts'un be se'óhket 'en.
5 K'an 'et 'aw dulh chanzoolhúch'e, k'us nuhwhudzi ndooda gunih, njan se'óhket huwa. Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en sba nduhoonla, nuhtsuhda dune hudoojih ka.
6 Njan nat nawhudezut keyah tube cho hoh dai whuzdli 'ink'ez nk'achah skwunlai 'i naoodutezulh whuts'un 'aw yun ts'oogaz k'us ha'ts'unoodzaih ghait'ah.
7 'Et Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en nuhts'un hasalh'a hoh hudoohjih ka, 'ink'ez yun k'ut dune niz de hedoh 'en chah huba. 'Ink'ez nuhwhutelhyih njan hooncha whehanwhutesdlelh.
8 Hoh k'an 'et 'aw nuhwheni ilah ndez sutelh'a, 'et whunts'i Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en 'usinla. Pharaoh be'abá sulhtsi 'ink'ez bukoo chah ba ts'iyawh whumoodih sili, 'ink'ez Egypt yun k'ut nduhoolhcho hoh huba ne'dusni sulhtsi.
9 'A ts'ih hoh 'ink'ez se'abá buts'un nahdulh. 'Ink'ez njan ndudoohni, ‘Joseph 'en nye' njan ndi nyulhni, “Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en Egypt nduhoolhcho ba moodih sulhtsi. Ndez suts'un inyalh, 'aw tsodilnik chaílah.
10 Goshen keyah 'et nioonásih, 'ink'ez 'et sih sugha ont'e, nyun, nyuzke, nyuzke hubuzke, nghunai, nuwhumusdus, 'ink'ez dant'i uht'i, 'i chah ts'iyawh.
11 'Et sih nuhwhula 'oosneh, khun teh nyun 'ink'ez nkoo hudelhts'i, 'ink'ez dant'i aht'i, 'i bulh nawhulh tewhúl'en whuzdli t'eh, nk'achah skwunlat nawhudutezulh whuts'un, tube cho hoh dai whutele hoh 'uhoont'ah.”’
12 'Ink'ez whunulh'en, nuhwhuna 'ink'ez Benjamin suchistl'ah buna be whunul'en, suzek be nuhwhulh yalhtuk hoh.
13 Hoh 'et se'abá njan ts'iyawh ndudoohni, dahoolhcho whudézti hoh Egypt 'et 'ust'en, ts'iyawh whunelh'en 'et. 'Ink'ez 'a ts'ih 'oohneh, se'abá 'en ndez toolhte.”
14 'Et hoh buchistl'ah Benjamin 'en yuts'ilchun k'elts'ut, 'ink'ez nadutso. Benjamin 'en chah yuts'ilchun k'ut nadutso.
15 'Et 'o 'un chah bulhutsinkah ts'iyawh nabutdunants'ooz 'ink'ez hubughu nadutse, 'et hukw'elh'az bulhutsinkah hiyulh yalhtuk.
16 K'an hoh Pharaoh bukoo hudelhts'i whulh uduzts'ai njan nduhutni, “Joseph bulhutsinkah 'en whusahandel.” Hoh Pharaoh ba soo 'uhooja 'ink'ez yunane'hut'en 'en cha huba hoonzoo.
17 Pharaoh 'en Joseph 'uyulhni, “Nyulhutsinkah njan ndo budoni, ‘Nuhghunai soo cho hoh buk'ena'oohlelh 'ink'ez natoohdelh yun k'ut Canaan ts'un.
18 Nuhwhe'abá 'en 'ink'ez nuhkoo delhts'i chah, 'en sts'un hatoohdelh. Egypt whuyun k'ut soo deghunla hoonzoo 'et nuhgha whutes'alh, 'ink'ez keyah whuk'o 'i tehyi.
19 K'an nuhba nuhts'uhezya, 'et ndóhneh, Egypt whuyun k'ut bunulbas, 'i be 'utoolhbas, nuhwhuzkehkah 'ink'ez nuh'atkah be, nuhwhe'abá 'udez toolhte 'ink'ez dez toohdelh.
20 'Ink'ez 'aw dant'i be ne'aht'en 'i 'aw huwuni chanuzáhzut. Egypt whuyun k'ut k'us hoonzoo 'et nuhwhe'hooldzun whutele.’”
21 'Et hoh Israel buye'kah 'et nduhúja. Joseph nulbas hubutl'ainla nts'e na'a Paraoh 'obulhni, whuz na'a, 'ink'ez ts'uyi chah hubut'unaidanla hutedulh wheni.
22 'Et ts'iyawh hubugha' ninla, 'ilhunnah dune hinli hoh, naih lhk'enahedutellelh 'i, 'et whunts'i Benjamin 300 k'ah silver 'ink'ez skwunlat ye soo nadutetnilh, 'i yugha ininla.
23 'Ink'ez be'abá 'en ndi 'i yuts'otl'ayanla: lanezi donkey soo yuntsih 'elhdulh dant'i unzoo, Egypt ts'un hooni 'i, 'ink'ez lanezi donkey ts'ekoo 'i haniyeh, lhes 'ink'ez ts'uyi be'abá títeyalh yuteyi wheni.
24 Hoh 'et bulhutsinkah whenaoobalh'a 'ink'ez whenáhedel. 'Ink'ez 'obulhni, “Whuz nahdulh hoh lhedoohdulh gunih.”
25 'Et Egypt huwanch'a duk whenahedel 'ink'ez Canaan yun k'ut whuz hube'abá Jacob buts'un lhgha nahudidel.
26 'Et njan nduhiyulhni, “Joseph nk'achah khuna 'ink'ez Egypt whuyun k'ut ts'iyawh huba moodih unli.” 'Ink'ez Jacob budzi 'ilhun 'uja, 'aw hiba 'alha cha'dinil 'et huwa.
27 'Et whunts'i Joseph dabudani 'et ts'iyawh hiyulh nawhelnuk, 'ink'ez ndi nulbas 'i yunalh'en Joseph yuts'otl'ayantan ye hitoolhbas ba, hoh k'an zeh hube'abá Jacob buzul naghutnah nasli.
28 Hoh Israel 'utni, “'Aw 'et soo 'uhooja, Joseph suye' nk'achah khuna. Buts'un tesyalh 'ink'ez ntelh'ilh datesah whutsah.”
