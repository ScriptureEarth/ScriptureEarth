Adam ts'u haindekah
5
Ndi dustl'us 'i Adam ts'uhainde hukw'e'eduguz 'int'ah. Ndet dzin Yak'usda (Elohim) dune inla da, Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en k'una'a yuk'edalhtsi.
2 'En 'obinla dune 'ink'ez ts'ekoo chah hoh soo 'obulhni. 'Ink'ez 'et dzin 'obinla whuts'un dune hubúlhni.
3 'Et hoh Adam 'en 130 be nawhudezut 'et yinkak usda hoh, buye' 'en duk'una'a whuzdli, 'en Seth hiyulhni.
4 Buye' Seth hooni hukw'elh'az dzin hoh Adam 'en 800 nawhudezut 'et yinkak usda hoh buye'kah 'ink'ez butse'kah chah huhooni.
5 'Et Adam 'en 'ilho yinkak inda hoh 930 nawhudezut hoh dazsai.
6 Seth 'en 105 nawhudezut 'et buye' Enosh 'en hooni.
7 Enosh 'en hooni hukw'elh'az Seth 'en 807 nawhudezut hoh 'et buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
8 'Et Seth 'en 'ilho yinkak inda hoh 112 nawhudezut hoh dazsai.
9 Enosh 'en 90 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh buye' Cainan 'en whuzdli.
10 Cainan 'en hooni hukw'elh'az, 'et Enosh 'en 815 nawhudezut hoh yinkak inda, 'et buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
11 'Et Enosh 'en 'ilho yinkak inda 'et 905 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh dazsai.
12 Cainan 'en 70 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh buye' Mahalalel hooni.
13 Mahalalel 'en hooni hukw'elh'az Cainan 'en 840 nawhudezut hoh buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
14 'Et Cainan 'en 'ilho yinkak inda, 'et 910 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh dazsai.
15 Mahalalel 'en 65 nawhudezut hoh buye' Jared 'en hooni.
16 Jared 'en hooni hukw'elh'az, Mahalalel 'en 830 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh 'et buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
17 'Et Mahalalel 'en 'ilho yinkak inda, 'et 895 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh dazsai.
18 Jared 'en 162 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh buye' Enoch 'en hooni.
19 Enoch hooni hukw'elh'az Jared 'en 800 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh, 'et buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
20 Jared 'en 'ilho yinkak inda, 'et 962 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh dazsai.
21 Enoch 'en 65 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh buye' Methuselah 'en hooni.
22 Methuselah hooni hukw'elh'az, Enoch 'en 300 nawhudezut whuts'un Yak'usda (Elohim) bulh nusúya, 'ink'ez buye'kah 'ink'ez butse'kah 'en huhooni.
23 Hoh 'et Enoch 'en 'ilho yinkak inda, 'et 365 nawhudezut yinkak inda suli.
24 Hoh Enoch 'en Yak'usda (Elohim) bulh nusíya 'ink'ez didut 'aw hoolah, Yak'usda (Elohim) nayilchoot 'et huwa.
25 Methuselah 'en 187 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh Lamech 'en hooni.
26 Lamech 'en hooni hukw'elh'az, 782 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh 'et buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
27 Methuselah 'en 'ilho yinkak inda hoh 969 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh dazsai.
28 Lamech 'en 182 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh buye' hooni.
29 'En Noah yulhni. Ndi boozi 'i ndez na'a ni hoh 'utni, “Ndunah 'en nesoo ne'oolh'in ba ne'ut'en 'i gha, 'ink'ez nela be nets'ut'en 'et chah, ndi yun k'ut chah Moodihti (Yahweh) neba hoontsi' whulhtsi 'et huwa.”
30 Noah hooni hukw'elh'az Lamech 'en 595 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh 'et buye'kah 'inkez butse'kah chah huhooni.
31 Hoh Lamech 'en 'ilho yinkak inda, 'et 777 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh dazsai.
32 'Ink'ez Noah 'en 500 nawhudezut yinkak inda hoh Noah buye'kah Shem, Ham, 'ink'ez Jepheth huhooni.
