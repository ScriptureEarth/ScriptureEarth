Hubudayicho Pharaoh 'en nusuti
41
'Et hoh lhawhuzut, nat nawhudezut whegha, 'et Pharaoh nusuti 'ink'ez whunilh'en took'oh whukw'abah 'et seyen.
2 Khunyaz skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus 'i njan took'oh 'et tooz hailh'az. Soo nul'en 'ink'ez nelhuk'a hoh tl'o k'ut hoh nudul'as.
3 'Et whunilh'en, skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus 'uyoo 'i chah yuk'ah tooz hailh'az. Ntsi' nul'en 'ink'ez 'uts'un zeh unli hoh 'et ndi musdus 'uyoo 'i yugha nudelhúya took'oh hukw'abah 'et.
4 'Ink'ez ndi ntsi' 'ink'ez 'uts'un zeh unli 'i ndi skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus soo nul'en 'ink'ez nelhuk'a, 'i ts'iya yanyi. 'Et Pharaoh ts'enandzut.
5 'Et nanesti 'ink'ez whulh nat nasuti, 'ink'ez khunyaz skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh tl'o tsih 'i lhuk'i duchun unli hoh dunicha 'ink'ez dinzoo.
6 'Et whunilh'en skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh tl'o tsih, dindot ndaz de nilhts'i be, 'ankw'us dezgi, 'i yuk'ah hanusdúki.
7 'Ink'ez skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh tl'o tsih dindot, 'i skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh tl'o tsih dincha 'ink'ez dinzoo, 'i ts'iyawh yudanyi. Hoh Pharaoh ts'enandzut, 'alha hoh nusuti.
8 K'an 'et bundada hoh lhawhuzut hoh bunududeh be ndi yunansuti 'i 'aw ba soo chahoonil, 'ink'ez wheoobalh'a 'ink'ez ts'iyawh 'et ndunnah ndi hubulhtah 'et ndi bunute gha nus dawhutenilh 'et hukw'anahuhulhye, 'en Egypt whut'en 'ink'ez ts'iyawh dune whunih 'en hubuka'dani. Hoh Pharaoh 'en njan whunanute 'et ndo bulhni. 'Ink'ez 'aw 'ilhunah whunts'i yuba hukw'un nuchahilhyel Pharaoh ba.
9 'Et ndunah dayi ne'ut'en 'en Pharaoh 'uyulhni, “K'an dzin hoh si suk'uz 'et njan whunalhunik.
10 Pharaoh 'en hinane'ut'en hubulh hunilch'e da, 'ink'ez si 'et whuz tsak'edelhts'i koo 'et hughoostan ka 'et nisunilhti inle 'ink'ez ndunah dayi 'ulht'es 'en chah.
11 'Anedult'et hoh 'ilha 'ulh'ek 'et nusidutez, didut 'ink'ez si chah. 'Ilhunah nidudli hoh njan 'et dawhutenilh 'et whunansidutez.
12 'Et njan Hebrew dune 'et newhulh 'int'ah tsak'esda hoh tsak'edelhtsi 'en hubugheztan 'en ndudidutni. Hoh njan whunansidutez 'et 'ilhunah nidudli hoh neba hukw'un hélhya.
13 'Ink'ez 'et hukw'elh'az nts'e na'a danwhulhni hoh hukw'un helhya 'et soo whuz na'a nduhooja. Si hoh njan se'ut'en 'et danasunilhti, 'ink'ez dayi 'ulht'es didut 'en buze'usduguz.”
14 Hoh Pharaoh 'en yuka whenáholh'a Joseph 'en yuka' dani hoh 'ats'ih whuz hahiyalhti tsak'et whuz 'ink'ez nahinanzo, hinaih chah lhk'enahidanla hoh Pharaoh ts'un lhgha dinya.
15 'Et Pharaoh 'en Joseph 'uyulhni, “Njan whunansiti 'et 'aw 'ilhunah whunts'i sba hukw'un hoolhye ghait'ah. 'Et 'usts'utni nyun ndi ba nants'utes 'et nt'eninzun hoh soo buk'ah ts'ih buk'unahilhye.”
16 'Et Joseph 'en Pharaoh 'uyulhni, “'Aw si ilah. Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en Pharaoh yuba soo 'udooni.”
17 Hoh Pharaoh 'en Joseph 'uyulhni, “Whunilh'en, subulhtah nasiti 'et took'oh whukw'abah 'et siyen.
18 Khunyaz skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus 'i njan took'oh 'et tooz hailh'az. Soo nul'en 'ink'ez nelhuk'a hoh tl'o k'ut nudul'as.
19 'Et whunilh'en 'uyoo skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus 'i chah yuk'ah tooz hailh'az, tube budaiyanghan. Tube chah ntsi' nul'en 'ink'ez 'uts'un zeh unli. Egypt nduhoolhcho keyah 'aw ndulhtsi' chases'en.
20 'Ink'ez ndi 'uts'un zeh unli 'ink'ez ntsi' nul'en musdus, 'i 'udechoo skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus 'i ts'iya hubanyi.
21 'Ink'ez ts'iya hubanyi hukw'elh'az 'aw whe chah nt'ets'unoozeh ghait'ah. Ts'iya hubanyi hoh 'et zeh 'uhilhtsi', soo 'udechoo da hunul'en 'et. 'Et hoh ts'enanusdzut.
22 'Ink'ez 'et doo chah subulh tah 'et whunulh'en 'ink'ez khunyaz skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh 'i ndi tl'o chun 'ilhuk'i k'ut hanalhyi, kenelhyi 'ink'ez dinzoo.
23 Hoh whunilh'en, skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh butsi 'i yudatsuz, dindot 'ink'ez ndaz de nilhtsi be dintsi' suli, 'i yuk'ah hanusduki.
24 'Ink'ez ndi dindot 'utsi 'i skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh 'utsi dinzoo 'i ts'iya wheyellhah. 'Et ndunnah ndi hubulhtah 'et ndi bunute gha nus dawhutenilh 'et hukw'anahuhulhye 'en ndo hubudusni. Hoh 'aw 'ilhunah whunts'i sts'un huba nachahiyel.”
25 'Et Joseph 'en Pharaoh 'uyulhni, “Pharaoh bubul 'i 'lhuk'i zeh 'int'ah. Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en Pharaoh 'aw 'etyaz nts'e daooténilh 'et whenúlhtun.
26 Skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus nzoo 'i skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut 'int'ah, 'ink'ez skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh 'utsi ninzoo 'i skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut 'int'ah. Ndi bulh bunansinti 'i 'ilhuk'i zeh 'int'ah.
27 'Ink'ez skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh musdus budayanghan 'ink'ez ntsi' nulh'en 'i yuk'ah halh'az 'et skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut. 'Ink'ez skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh ndaz de nilhtsi be butsi dintsi' be dizgi suli, 'et skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut hoh dai whutele, ni hoh 'utni.
28 Njan 'et Pharaoh ts'un hoh bulh yalhtuk, Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en Pharaoh wheyunelhtan nts'e na'a datenilh 'et ba.
29 'Alha 'et skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut hoh Egypt keyah nduhoolhcho hoh tube cho hoh ha'unteyeh whutele.
30 'Et whunts'i skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut 'et hukw'elh'az teh, tube cho dai whutele, 'ink'ez ts'iyawh ndi lhai njan keyah Egypt 'et whunahodutelnah. 'Ink'ez ndi tube cho dai 'i be keyah ts'iya whutele.
31 'Et ndi lhai hananyi, 'i 'aw nt'echantesdzin njan keyah 'uhoont'ah. Ndi dai cho 'i hukw'edutezulh 'et huwa 'ink'ez tube cho hoh dai whutele.
32 'Ink'ez ndi Pharaoh yunansuti 'i nat hoh yuba yuk'unhelhya, Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en 'ilhuz njan ndo whutelilh 'et huwa. 'Ink'ez Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en 'aw 'etyaz ndo telilh.
33 K'an hoh Pharaoh 'en dune soo buna be whunooh whunilh'en 'ink'ez whunih 'en 'utahaitelhtelh 'ink'ez Egypt yun k'ut 'et ba moodih tele.
34 Pharaoh 'et njan ndoneh 'ink'ez ndan yun k'ut ba ne'duteni 'en 'utahayoolhtelh. Njan bulah skwunlai whe 'ilhuk'i (⅕) njan Egypt yun k'ut haneyeh 'i 'ilhun niyoolelh skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh lhai nawhudutezulh whuts'un.
35 'Ink'ez 'et ndi ts'uyi 'i ts'iyawh 'ilhah highoollelh njan hoonzoo nawhuduteszulh 'et, 'ink'ez wheyunududzaih 'i ts'iyawh 'ilhun ninoodzaih, Pharaoh 'en huba ne'duni hoh, 'ink'ez ts'uyi 'i ts'iyawh ts'uwhut'i whucho lhelhdooh 'et nihiyoolelh.
36 'Ink'ez 'et ndi ts'uyi 'i njan keyah huba 'utet'e skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh dai nawhudutezulh wheni yun k'ut Egypt 'et. Hoh sih 'et keyah hubudachayoosghan, dai cho whuzli t'eh.”
Joseph huba ne'duni suli
37 'Et njan be bughazdani 'i Pharaoh buna ts'un ba hoonzoo 'ink'ez hilane'duni 'en chah.
38 Pharaoh 'en hilane'duni 'en 'obulhni, “'Aw ih ndunah 'en naoodultelh ghait'ah? Dune 'en ndan Yak'usda (Elohim) bunududeh 'en yulh 'int'ah 'en?”
39 Hoh Pharaoh 'en Joseph ndo bulhni, “Njan whunilh'en Yak'usda (Elohim) njan ts'iyawh whunyunelhtan, 'aw 'ilhunah whunts'ih soo buna be whunooh whunilh'en 'ink'ez whunih chah hoolah, nyun zeh ndunint'ah.
40 Sukoo 'et huba ne'doni 'ink'ez sudunekah 'en ts'iyawh nghunek 'i datni, 'et hik'ah ts'ih 'uhoot'en. Njan kw'usudacho buk'ut 'et zeh ngha nus 'utest'e.”
41 'Ink'ez Pharaoh 'en Joseph 'ulhni, “Whunilh'en lhe'whusdla, Egypt yun k'ut ndulhcho 'et nla k'ehola.”
42 Hoh Pharaoh 'en bulande'ai, 'i be moodih unli, 'i ts'uhayet'ai, 'ink'ez Joseph yula nida'ai, hoh naih unzoo 'i ye soo naididla 'ink'ez tsi nizdelya gold 'i yuts'ilchun nidanla.
43 'Ink'ez njan yeztli yelhbus 'i buk'ah élhbus yit'i 'i yetl'ai dalhda, 'ink'ez 'et hits'un bubut sulyehudulhdulh, “Nuhgwut ts'i be nadelhúya!” 'Et Egypt yun k'ut nduhóolhcho ba 'et ninilhti.
44 Pharaoh 'en Joseph 'uyulhni, “Pharaoh si 'ust'ah, 'et huwa 'aw nyun a'ah chadinil t'eh dune 'ilhunah whunts'i bula be didoolnih, 'ink'ez buke chah be 'aw Egypt whuyun k'ut 'et nadoot'es ghait'ah.”
45 'Et Pharaoh 'en Joseph yoozi 'uhoonla hoh Zaphnath-Paaneah yulhni, 'ink'ez bu'at ba 'en Asenath yugha inilhti, ts'uwhut'i whut'en On huba nawhulnuk 'en Poti-Pherah butse'. 'Et Joseph 'en yun k'ut Egypt nduhoolhcho hoh ba 'et nininya.
46 Joseph 'en tat lanezi nawhudezut hoh Egypt whudayicho Pharaoh 'en yubut seyin. 'Ink'ez Joseph 'en Pharaoh bunalh hoh 'un whenya 'ink'ez Egypt whuyun k'ut 'et dutsuk hainya.
47 K'an hoh skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut 'et lhai haniyeh, 'i njan yun k'ut tube cho lhai hoh hanalhyi whuzli.
48 'Et hoh ts'iyawh ts'uyi, dant'i k'an hiyendunidzit cha ílah, 'i 'ilhuhighezdla skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut whuts'un njan Egypt yun k'ut 'et, 'ink'ez ts'uyi 'i ts'uwhut'i whucho totsuk 'et lhk'ehiyinla. Ts'uwhut'i whucho totsuk 'et lhk'ehiyelya whutl'o k'uz hubunat haneyeh 'i.
49 Joseph tube lhai hoh wheyunududzaih 'i 'ilhighezdzai, yatoo sai k'una'a. Oodulhtoh istle' deja whuts'un, 'aw whe hoh ts'oodólhto ghait'ah.
50 Joseph buye'kah nanah, dai whutele nawhudutezulh whutsah, ndun Asenath 'en Poti-Pherah butse', ts'uwhut'i On hubunawhulnuk 'en 'uski ut'i.
51 Joseph 'en 'udetsah Manasseh yulhni, “Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en dant'i be tena'elhú'en, 'ink'ez se'abá bukoo 'et ts'iyawh whunaoodelhúnah sulhtsi.”
52 'Ink'ez whulh nat buye' 'en Ephraim yulhni, “Njan yun k'ut dzuh nusízut inle, k'an hoh Yak'usda (Elohim) 'en suba whulai whulhtsi.”
53 'Et skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh 'unullai nawhudezut yun k'ut Egypt 'et ustle 'uhooja.
54 'Ink'ez skwunlai 'o 'at nankoh nawhudezut dai cho whutele 'et whe'whudit'en. Joseph ntse na'a ndutni inle, 'et ndi dai cho 'i yun k'ut dutsuk nidenzut, 'et whunts'i Egypt yun k'ut nduhóolhcho 'et lhes hooni.
55 Hoh Egypt keyah nduhoolhcho hubudayanghan, dune 'en Pharaoh ts'un tso bulh hoh lhes ka 'uhutni. 'Et Pharaoh 'en Egypt whut'en 'obulhni, “Joseph 'en buts'un ahdulh. Daja nuhwhulhni 'et ndohneh.”
56 Ndi dai cho 'i yun k'ut hukw'ut ndulhcho nidenzut, 'ink'ez Joseph 'en koo uzdla totsuk 'et duni'denla, 'ink'ez Egypt whut'en ts'un ye'onket. 'Ink'ez ndi dai cho 'i yun k'ut Egypt tube be 'uhooja.
57 Yun k'ut totsuk whut'en 'en Egypt yun k'ut whuz Joseph hits'un lhgha dindel haniyeh hootekulh ka, dai cho 'i yun k'ut nduhoolhcho hoh be 'uhooja 'et huwa.
