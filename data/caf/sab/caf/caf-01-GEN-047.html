47
Hoh Joseph 'en whenya 'ink'ez Pharaoh 'en nduyulhni, “Se'abá, 'ink'ez se'ilhunahkah, hubusheep, hubumusdus, 'ink'ez dant'i hiyit'i 'i bulh keyah Canaan whuz hahandel. 'Ink'ez 'alha hoh keyah Goshen 'et nihunindel.”
2 'Ink'ez skwunlatnah be'ilhunahkah 'en 'utahabanla 'ink'ez 'en Pharaoh ts'un lhgha budinla.
3 'Et Pharaoh 'en be'ilhunahkah 'obulhni, “Danhoont'ah 'et whene'aht'en?” 'Et Pharaoh 'en 'uhiyulhni, “Nyunane'hut'en 'en sheep gheztan 'uts'int'ah, 'anedult'et nuwheni 'ink'ez nuwhe'abákah chah.”
4 'Et hoh Pharaoh 'en 'uhiyulhni, “Keyah 'et nitadutnilh, nyenane'hut'en 'en 'aw khunai whenudelhuya chahoot'ih bughunai ba. Keyah Canaan ndi dai cho 'i be tube 'uhooja. K'an 'et hoh nela'ineh nyunane'ut'enkah 'en keyah Goshen 'et nihoona ka bugha ho'alh.”
5 Pharaoh 'en Joseph yulh yailhtuk hoh njan ndutni, “Nye'abá 'ink'ez nye'ilhunahkah 'en nts'un hunindel.
6 Egypt whuyun k'ut 'et nuhbut whuz'ai, nye'abá 'ink'ez nye'ilhunahkah, keyah ndet k'us hoonzoo 'et nihoona ka' 'obonleh, keyah Goshen 'et nihoona. 'Ink'ez ndan soo khunai gheztan nt'eooninzun hubutah, 'en sughunai ba degha nus ne'duni huboolhtsilh.”
7 Hoh Joseph 'en be'abá Jacob 'en Pharaoh yebut ninilhti, 'ink'ez Jacob 'en Pharaoh hoonzoo 'obulhni yulhtsi.
8 Pharaoh 'en Jacob 'uyulhni, “Dahoolhdzah hoh yinkak inda?”
9 'Et Jacob 'en Pharaoh 'uyulhni, “Súnawhudezut whe dzin hoh lhusiya 'et 130 nawhudezut. 'Aw whulai ilah 'ink'ez hoontsi' hoh nawhudezut whudzin hoh suyust'e 'ant'e, 'ink'ez 'aw chahahoolil budzin se'abákah lhusiya bunawhudezut 'et.”
10 'Et hoh Jacob 'en Pharaoh hoonzoo 'obulhni yulhtsi, 'ink'ez yuch'a 'un whenaja.
11 Joseph 'en be'abá 'ink'ez be'ilhunahkah 'en soo ts'ih nibuninla, 'ink'ez Egypt whuyun k'ut 'et hubutl'aho'ai, keyah k'us hoonzoo 'et, Rameses keyah, Pharaoh 'en huba'hoonla hoh.
12 Hoh Joseph 'en be'abá, be'ilhunahkah, 'ink'ez ts'iyawh be'abá bukoo dune hinli, 'en chah 'en ndi lhes huba dahunelts'uk hinli hukw'u na'a hubugha' ni'ai.
Joseph dai cho whuzli 'et hukw'un nuhulhye
13 K'an keyah nduhoolhcho hoh lhes chahoolel. Ndi dai cho whuzdli, 'i tube 'uhooja hoh Egypt whuyun k'ut 'ink'ez Canaan whuyun k'ut 'et 'ants'i hoh nahukat li'hut'en suli, ndi dai cho nadanzut 'et huwa.
14 Joseph 'en sooneya ts'iyawh 'ilhinighez'ai, Egypt whuyun k'ut naini'ai, 'i 'ink'ez Canaan whuyun k'ut 'et chah. Haneyeh hiyóoket 'i 'ink'ez Joseph sooneya 'i Pharaoh bukoo ts'un dainendzai.
15 Hoh 'et ndi sooneya ts'iya suli, Egypt whuyun k'ut 'ink'ez Canaan whuyun k'ut chah. Egypt whut'en Joseph ts'un lhgha hudindel 'ink'ez 'uhutni, “Lhes negha ni'aih, dant'i ka nyunalh 'et yats'odlah? Nezooneya ts'iya suli.”
16 'Et Joseph 'utni, “Nuhghunai 'i stl'ahle, 'ink'ez 'et lhes nugha oosdlelh nuhghunai k'elhla, nuhzooneya ts'iya suli t'eh.”
17 Hoh hubughunai Joseph ts'un lhgha hidinla 'ink'ez Joseph 'en ts'un lhes 'i hubuyeztli k'elha hitezdla, sheep chah, musdus chah, 'ink'ez hubudonkey chah. 'Et lhes huteyi 'et 'ilhah naoodezut whuts'un hubughunai k'elha.
18 'Et nawhudezut whulatah suli hoh, whulhnat nawhudezut 'et hits'un lhgha hudindel 'ink'ez 'uhutni, “'Aw nemoodih chawhuntodul'ih ghait'ah. Nezooneya ts'iya suli 'et, nemoodih 'en chah neghunai butl'ayadulya, 'aw dant'i nts'ih chasidut'ih suli nemoodih banalh hoh. Neyust'e 'ink'ez nuwhukeyah 'aw 'i zeh idut'i.
19 Dant'ih ka nyuna nalh yats'odlah, 'anedult'et nuwheni 'ink'ez nuwheyun k'et? Nehoonket 'ink'ez nuwheyun k'et chah, lhes k'elha, 'ink'ez 'et nuwheni 'ink'ez nuwheyun k'et 'et Pharaoh ba ne'hut'en odudle. Whuyunududzai 'i negha nondzaih, 'et sih hodutna 'ink'ez 'aw yachasodudla, 'et sih yun k'ut 'aw 'uhoolah chahosdle.”
20 Hoh Joseph 'en Egypt whuyun k'ut nduhoolhcho Pharaoh yuba honket. Egypt whut'en 'ilhunah hinli hoh hubutl'o k'ut, ndi dai cho 'i tube hubuk'edinzut 'et huwa. 'Ink'ez ndi yun k'ut ts'iyawh Pharaoh bube'ildzun suli.
21 'Ink'ez 'et whudunekah 'en nts'e whut'i whucho 'et nibuninla, Egypt whe 'udechoo whenaoodez'a 'ink'ez soo 'udek'elh'az whenaoodez'a whuts'un.
22 Nawhulnuk hubukeyah 'et zeh 'aw chahoookulh. Pharaoh 'en soo 'uneltsuk hubugha yu'aih, 'ink'ez hubugha íyut'aih 'i huyeyi, 'et huwa 'aw hubukeyah whecha'hookulh.
23 Joseph 'en dunekah ts'un 'utni, “'Alha hoh k'an dzin Pharaoh ba nuhoket, nuhwheni 'inkez nuhkeyah chah. Whunulh'en njan nuhba wheyunududzaih 'int'ah, 'ink'ez 'i be yun k'ut wheyunoohdzaih.
24 'Ink'ez hukw'elh'az hanahyi t'eh, 'et skwunlai whe 'ilhuki (⅕) 'i Pharaoh butl'ainoohdzaih. Bulh dit be skwunlai (⅘) 'i nuhwhenich'oh ba'oolhdzin, tl'o k'ut wheyunududzaih ba, 'ink'ez tehyi chah ba, nuhkoo delhts'i 'ink'ez nuhwhuzkeh ba ts'uyi chah.”
25 'Et njan nduhutni, “Nuwhezilhyih hoh khutadutna, nemoodih ts'un hoonzoo bunalh odut'e wule, 'ink'ez Pharaoh ba ne'hut'en 'odudle.”
26 Hoh Joseph 'en Egypt whuyun k'ut ba nahezya k'an dzin whuts'un, 'et nduhoont'ah, Pharaoh 'en skwunai whe'ilhuk'i (⅕) 'i buts'un 'oot'e. Nawhulnuk hubukeyah 'et whu'et, 'et 'aw Pharaoh be 'uhooldzun chaílah.
Joseph 'en Jacob ts'un ts'ih'un nahezya
27 Hoh Israel 'en Egypt whuyun k'et 'et whut'i, Goshen whukeyah 'et, 'ink'ez 'et 'uhít'i 'ink'ez hunézyi 'ink'ez lhk'uz teh hoh hulai suli.
28 'Et Jacob 'en Egypt whuyun k'et lanezi 'o 'at lhk'alti (17) nawhudezut hoh 'et dune inle. Jacob 'en 147 nawhudezut 'et yinkak inda.
29 'Aw 'et Jacob datetsah whegha suli hoh, buye' Joseph yukana'detni 'ink'ez ndi yulhni, “K'an hoh hoonzoo nyulh niooni'ai t'eh, sla ineh nyula 'i be sukechun yuh nidolnih 'ink'ez hoonzoo ts'ih 'ink'ez sladontnih gunih. Sla'inja hoh 'aw Egypt 'et wheyuchasuzolhtel.
30 Su'abákah hubutah 'et nisolhtelh. Egypt 'et whehanasolhtelh 'ink'ez nts'e whuyahudinla 'et whuyusolhtelh.” 'Ink'ez 'et ndi yulhni, “Nts'e na'a ndusudini, 'et sih ndosneh.”
31 'Ink'ez 'et 'utni, “Suts'un ts'ih'un nahinyeh.” Hoh 'et yuts'un ts'ih'un nahezya hoh Israel butsi be yuk 'uja, bukw'usuti tsi t'ah 'aw 'et.
