Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu
Teke o deru iya bụ g'onye mpfuchiru Chileke, bụ Nétanu byachaaru ibe iya bya epfuaru iya l'o meru ẹji l'o jepfuru Batusheba
51
Phụnaaru mu obu-imemini;
jiko Chileke;
eshinu n-yemobu ngu te
nwedu echihu.
Huchafunu atụpya ono,
mu tụpyaru ngu ono;
eshinu ịiphuje obu-imemini;
phụa k'etsutsu iya.
2 Sanaa mu rengurengu;
safụkota mu ẹjo-iphe,
mu megbabẹru;
l'i huchafụ mu iphe-ẹji,
mu meshiru ngu!
3 Kẹle mu maru gẹ mu
tụpyaberu ngu;
ọphu mu azọhajedu iphe-ẹji
mu azọha.
4 Ọo gụbedua gẹdegede bẹ
mu mesweru;
mbụ l'ọo gụbedua bẹ
mu meru iphe-ẹji.
Iswi dụ ngu mma l'ikpe,
i kperu mu;
ọphu ụta adụdu ngu iya l'ikpe,
ị nmarụ mu.
5 Mbụ-a; mu shikwa teke a
nwụru mu bụru onye
ẹjo-iphe.
Mbụ lẹ mu shirọ teke ne mu
tsụtaru ime mu bụtaru
onye eme iphe-ẹji.
6 Iphe, iile ẹnya iya lẹ ndzụ
amadụ bụ ire-lanụ.
Zinaa mu mmamiphe nkengu.
7 Gude ịsopu saa mu asasa
gẹ mu dụebe ree!
Saa mu gẹ mu chaa pemu
pemu g'aka-mini-sunoo!
8 Mee g'ẹhu tsọo mu ụtso;
gẹ mu tee ẹswa;
k'ọphu ọkpu mu ono,
ị nyajishiru ono
a-gbakerehu.
9 Gbakụtaru iphe-ẹji,
mu meshiru azụ!
L'i hufukọta ẹjo-iphe,
mu mekọtaru g'ọ ha!
10 Jiko Chileke; menaa g'obu
mu gụa mu ìphóró;
l'ị nụ mu unme ọphuu,
a-nọdu anụru ngu opfu.
11 Ta chịfukwa mu l'atatiphu
ngu!
Ọphu ị nafụkwa mu
Unme-dụ-Nsọ nkengu!
12 Nụ-phunu mu ẹhu-ụtso
ono azụ;
mbụ ẹhu-ụtso ono, shi l'adzọta,
ị dzọtaru mu ono.
Mee g'eme uche ngu heje mu
ihee; k'ọphu ọo-gbata
mu ike.
13 Ọo ya bụ gẹ mu ezia ndu
anọduje atụpya ngu nụ
ụzo ngu.
Ndu iphe-ẹji adakọbe
lwapfuta ngu.
14 Dzọnaa mu l'ikpe ọchi,
mu gburu; jiko Chileke!
L'ọo ngu bụ Chileke,
adzọje mu nụ.
Ọo ya bụ gẹ mu egude ire mu
gụa ebvu ẹhu-ụtso sụ
l'ị pfụberekoto.
15 Gụbe Nnajịuphu; kụhaa
mu ọnu;
k'ọphu mu a-ja ngu ajaja.
16 Ọme ọo ngwẹja bẹ iile
ẹnya iya; mẹ mu
egwekwaru iya
nụ ngu.
Obenu lẹ ngwẹja m'obeta ọ
bụru ngwẹja-akpọ-ọku
te emekwa g'ẹhu tsọo
ngu ụtso.
17 Kẹle ngwẹja ọphu Chileke
anatajẹ bụ aphụ-eji-obu.
Onye aphụ jiru obu l'iphe
o meru; izimanụ lwa iya
azụ bẹ gụbe Chileke ta
anmajẹkwa phulaphula.
18 Menaa Zayọnu g'ọ jaa àjàjà;
eshinu ọ dụ ngu l'obu.
Kpụnaa igbulọ Jierúsalẹmu!
19 Ọo ya bụ g'eegweaharu
ngu ngwẹja, pfụru ọto;
ngwẹja-akpọ-ọku
atsọahaa ngu ụtso;
l'a watarụ ngu egburu
oke-eswi l'ọru-ngwẹja
ngu.
