Ebvu-ọma eswe-atụta-unme
92
Ọo iphe, dụ ree g'a jajẹ
Chipfu ajaja;
mbụ g'e gudeje ebvu tua
gụbe
Ọkalibe-kakọta-shii ẹpha;
2 mbụ g'a gbẹshije l'ụtsu
je epfukashịaru ndiphe
kẹ n-yemobu ngu ono.
Teke o rwuru l'ẹnyashi;
eeje epfuaru phẹ g'ị
bụ-be onye apfụshije ike l'iphe,
i pfuru.
3 Iphe, ee-gude akụ iya lẹ
nkwa bụ une,
a kparụ l'ụdo iri;
yẹe ogelenda,
aha gengugengu;
4 kẹle gụbe Chipfu gudewaa
iphe, dụ ree, iimeje mee
g'ẹhu tsọo mu ụtso.
Mu agụje ebvu ẹhu-ụtso mẹ mu
hụmaepho iphe,
i gude ẹka
meshigbaa dobe.
5 Gụbe Chipfu; lewarọ g'iphe,
iime habe shii.
Tẹme ọriri ngu paa omilomi.
6 Obenu l'onye egomunggo
swehuru ta amadụ
l'onoo g'ọ dụ;
ọphu o dokwarụpho
onye-eswe ẹnya
ẹgube ono;
7 mbụ l'a makwarụ-a lẹ ndu
ẹjo-iphe akpọ-fushi
g'ẹka ẹswa efushi;
tẹme lẹ ndu iwashị bẹ
iphemiphe, ẹphe eme
bụkotaepho ụpete;
ọle ee-mechakwanaa
mebyia phẹ jasụ l'ojejoje.
8 Obenu lẹ gụbe Chipfu parụ
ẹka jasụ lẹ tuutuutuu
lẹ mịimiimii.
9 Gụbe Chipfu; ndu ọhogu ngu
e-mechakwaa bụru
mkpurupyata;
mbụ-a; ndu ẹjo-iphe g'ẹphe
hakọtakpoo bẹ
ee-mechakwaa
chịka nanụ nanụ.
10 Obenu l'i meru;
ọkpehu mu dụ gẹ k'atsụ.
Ị bya eworu manụ,
eshi mkpọ wụa mu l'ishi.
11 Mu gudewaa ẹnya mu hụma
lẹ ndu ọhogu mu phẹ
bẹ e mekperu;
bya egude nchị mu nụma
lẹ ndu ẹjo-iphe,
wulihuru etso mu opfu
bẹ a vụwaru
l'ọgbarabvu.
12 Ndu pfụberekoto a-nọdu
aja ajaja gẹ mpfụ.
Ẹphe a-nọdu evu evuvu
g'oshi sida k'úbvú
úbvú Lébanọnu,
13 e yeru l'ime ụlo
kẹ Chipfu.
Ẹphe a-nọdu l'ọkpoku Chileke
anyi asọ pulapula.
14 Ẹphe -kahụchakpoo akahụ;
ẹphe amịle-a mebyi.
Ẹphe adụele-a nwata nwata;
bya amaẹle-a rọoroo;
15 k'ọphu ẹphe e-shi nno
goshi lẹ Chipfu bụ onye
pfụberekoto;
bya abụru
agbara-mkpuma nkemu;
tẹme ọphu ọ dụdu iphe,
apfụduru-ọto,
oomeje.
