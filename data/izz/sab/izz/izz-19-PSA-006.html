Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu; g'e gude olu-edede gụa ya; g'a kpọru une ye iya
6
Gụbe Chipfu; te egudekwa
ẹhu-eghu baarụ mu
mba!
Te egudekwa ẹnya,
ata ngu kpịtikpiti hụ̀a
mu àhụ̀hù!
2 Phụaru mu obu-imemini
jiko Chipfu;
kẹle mu eyifuwa eyifu.
Mee g'ẹhu dụ mu ike;
kẹle ẹhu mu l'ophu
tụko aphụkota mu
kparịkpari.
3 Tẹme meji nọdu atọfukwa
mu phọ atọfu.
Sụ-a; gụbe Chipfu; ?bụkpoo a
-nọo jasụ teke ole
b'ịi-gbaru mu mkpu?
4 Lwaphutanụ azụ Chipfu;
bya agbafụta mu gẹ mu
dzụru ndzụ. Phụnaaru mu
obu-imemini; dzọ mu rọ!
5 Kẹle; onye nwụhuru nụ
ta anyataẹkwaru ngu.
?Bụ onye a-gbẹ l'alị-maa
ekele ngu ekele?
6 Mu tsụakwaru ude jasụ ike
bvụ mu.
Ẹnyashi mbọku-mbọku bẹ mu
azẹje l'iphe-azẹe mu
kwakọta ẹkwa
mkpụrumkpuru.
Ọphu ọ dụdu mbọku,
a nọghajeru g'ẹnya-mini
ta agba utso l'iphe,
mu azẹje azẹe.
7 Aphụ, tsọru mu nụ mewaru;
mu raa ẹkwa aphụ
ragaraga.
O mewaru; ọphu mu
aphụ-rweẹdu aphụ-rwe;
ọ bụru iphe, ndu achị mu
lẹ njọ kparụ.
8 Unu tụko parụ mu haa;
unubẹ ndu ono, eme iphe,
adụdu ree ono;
kẹle Chipfu nụmawaru
olu ẹkwa mu.
9 Chipfu nụmawaru arworwọ,
mu rwọru iya.
Epfupfu, mu epfu anụ iya
rwuwaru iya nchị.
10 G'e kpukwaa ndu ọhogu mu
iphu lẹ ntụ;
gẹ meji tọfukwa phẹ.
G'ẹphe tsẹkwaa iphu ẹgwegwa
gude iphere laphu azụ.
