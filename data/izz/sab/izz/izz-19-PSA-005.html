Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu; g'a phụru ototo ye iya
5
Jiko Chipfu; ngabẹnu nchị
nụma olu mu;
Jiko gẹ ude, mu atsụ rwua
ngu rọ l'ẹhu!
2 Gụbe Chileke mu, bụ onye
Eze mu;
ngabẹnu nchị nụma mkpu,
mu echi echiku ngu!
Noo kẹle ọo ngu bẹ mu epfu
anụ.
3 Gụbe Chipfu; ụtsu g'ụtsu bẹ
ịinumaje olu mu.
Mu ewojeru mkpa mu
dooru ngu giriri; daa
ngabẹru ngu ụtsu g'ụtsu.
4 Chileke; ị tị bụdu onye
ẹjo-iphe adụje ree.
Ọphu iikwejedu gẹ ndu eme
ẹjo-ememe nọ-kube
ngu.
5 Ndu eku onwophẹ ta adụdu
ike apfụru l'iphu ngu.
Iphe, bụkpoo ndu eme iphe,
adụdu ree bẹ dụkota
ngu ashị.
6 Iphe, bụkpoo ndu anọduje
adzụ ụka bẹ iimeje
g'ẹphe bụru mkpurupyata.
Ndu egbu ọchi; yẹe ndu ụgho
bẹ gụbe Chipfu anọduje
akpọfu akpọfu.
7 Obenu lẹ mbẹdua a-byajẹ
l'ụlo ngu;
eshinu i yeru mu obu.
Ọ bụru ntsumebvu bẹ mu
e-gudeje gha iphu
l'eze-ụlo ngu, dụ nsọ
baarụ ngu ẹja.
8 Kpọ-ziaru mu ụzo jiko Chipfu
eshinu ị bụ onye
pfụberekoto.
Kpọ-zinaaru mu ụzo;
m'ọbvu ndu achị mu
lẹ njọ.
Menaa g'ụzo, ịicho gẹ mu
shia doo mu ẹnya.
9 Ọ tọ dụdu opfu, shi phẹ l'ọnu
mẹ nanụ, e gude ẹka.
Obu phẹ bụ iwashị tụkoru
iya sweta.
Ire phẹ bẹ ẹphe egudeje aja
nwibe phẹ ajaja;
obenu l'iphe, nọ phẹ l'ime
obu anọduje eshi
ishi g'ilu, gheru ọnu.
10 Jiko Chileke; menaa g'iphe-ẹji,
ẹphe eme tukoru phẹ l'ishi!
G'idzu, ẹphe chịru nmakwaa
phẹ g'ọ́nyà.
Chịshichaa phẹ Chileke;
noo kẹle ẹphe
kwefunụkawaru ngu
ike;
tẹme ẹphe nọdu anọduje
atụpya ngu tekenteke.
11 Obenu-a; g'ẹhu-ụtso bụkwaru
kẹ ndu agbabajẹ l'ime
ngu je anọdu
zeeru ndzụ.
G'ẹphe gụje ebvu ẹhu-ụtso
jasụ lẹ tuutuutuu lẹ
mịimiimii.
Gbochitenu phẹ Chileke!
G'iphe, bụkpoo ndu yeru
ẹpha ngu obu etee ẹswa.
12 Noo kẹle ọo gụbe Chipfu
agọjeru ọnu-ọma nụ
ndu pfụberekoto.
Iigudeje l'ọo l'ẹphe dụ ngu
l'obu gbobuta phẹ.
