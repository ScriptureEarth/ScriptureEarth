Ebvu, Dévidi deru
37
G'ọnodu ndu eme ẹjo-ememe
te eghujekwa ngu eghu;
ọphu i kojekwaru ndu
apfụbeduru ẹka ọto
okophoo.
2 Kẹle ọ tọ dụkwa
g'aa-nọ-beru; ẹphe
anwụlwashihu g'ẹswa;
tẹme ọphu ọ dụkwapho
g'aa-nọ-beru;
ẹphe akpọshihu nkụ
g'irwu oshi.
3 Dakobe Chipfu; meje iphe,
dụ ree!
Buru l'alị ono bụru
o-me-g'o-pfuru!
4 Nọdukwa Chipfu l'ẹka tee
ẹswa;
g'ọo-nụkwanu ngu iphe,
bụ iphe, agụ ngu nụ.
5 Woru iphemiphe, iime
ememe ye Chipfu l'ẹka.
Dakobe iya; l'oo-meru ngu
iya.
6 Oo-me g'apfụbekoto nkengu
nwua kẹ phoo g'ìphóró;
mbụ lẹ Chipfu a-harụ
ngu enge;
mee g'iswi-ọdu-mma
nkengu gbua kẹ nwịinwii
g'ẹnyanwu.
7 Zẹkpoepho kụpya l'ẹka
Chipfu; taa nshi kwabẹru
iya.
G'ẹhu te eghukwa ngu
eghu teke iphemiphe,
ndu ẹjo-iphe eme
ejehuru phẹ;
mbụ teke ẹjo-idzu, ẹphe achị
anọduje eje g'ẹphe chịru iya.
8 G'ẹhu te eghujekwa ngu eghu;
ọphu ị vọjekwaru ọku
wụa ẹhu.
G'ẹhu te eghujekwa ngu eghu;
anọnya i mee ẹjo-iphe.
9 Kẹle ndu anọduje eme
ẹjo-ememe bẹ ee-me
g'ẹphe chịhu; obenu
l'ọo ndu daru ngabẹru
Chipfu bẹ e-nweru
mgboko.
10 Ọ tọ dụdu g'ọo-nọ-beru
l'e mee gẹ ndu ẹjo-iphe
ta adụhe.
Ii-le ẹnya phẹ; ọle ị tịi
hụmaedu phẹ.
11 Ọo ndu dụ àgù bẹ mgboko-a
a-bụru nkephẹ;
tẹme ẹphe enweru ẹhu-guu;
nweru iya k'etsutsu iya.
12 Ndu ẹjo-iphe achị ẹjo
idzu edoberu ndu
pfụberekoto;
ataru phẹ ikireze kẹ
nyịkinyiki.
13 Ọlobuele-a ọchi bẹ Chipfu
achị phẹ;
kẹle o maru lẹ teke
ee-mebyishi phẹ l'abya-a.
14 Ndu ẹjo-iphe mịtaru ogu-echi
phẹ; g'ẹphe gbushia
ndu ụzo phẹ pfụru
nhamụnha;
bya eseta apfụ phẹ kẹ kịkiki
g'ẹphe gbatsushia ndu
ụkpa waa ndu adụdu
g'ọ dụ phẹ.
15 Obenu l'ogu-echi phẹ
ono e-mechaa nmabaa
phẹ ẹpho;
l'a nata phẹ apfụ phẹ
ono nyakwoshia.
16 Nwiphe, abadu ishi,
ndu pfụberekoto nweru
bẹ kakwa ree eme l'ẹku,
ndu ẹjo-iphe dụ igwerigwe
kpaa kụbe.
17 Kẹle ndu ẹjo-iphe bẹ aa-nafụ
iphe, ẹphe gude eme ike;
obenu lẹ ndu pfụberekoto
bẹ Chipfu bụ agbarike phẹ.
18 Chipfu maru gẹ ndu ụta
adụdu l'ẹhu a-nọ-beru.
Okiphe phẹ a-nọ jasụ lẹ
tuutuutuu lẹ mịimiimii.
19 Teke eeje iphe-ẹhuka;
bẹ a ta abyadụ ekpu phẹ
iphu lẹ ntụ;
tẹme ọkpa nri -byaẹpho;
nri adụele phẹ-a
shingushingu.
20 Obenu lẹ ndu ẹjo-iphe
a-bụru mkpurupyata.
Ndu ọhogu Chipfu
e-phekashịhu nanụ nanụ
g'okoko, nọ l'ẹgu.
Ẹphe e-phukashịhu
g'ẹnwuru-ọku.
21 Ndu ẹjo-iphe abụjeru;
ẹphe -jita ụgwo ẹphe
eria ya.
Obenu lẹ ndu pfụberekoto
bẹ anọduje emeru
ndiphe iphe-ọma.
22 Ọo ndu Chipfu gọru
ọnu-ọma nụ e-nweru mgboko-a;
obenu lẹ ndu ọ tụkwanuru
ọnu bẹ a-chịhu nụ.
23 Ọo Chipfu anọduje akpọ-ziru
nemadzụ ụzo g'oo-gudeje
aha ọkpa; mee onye ono
g'ọ ngụru angụru;
mbụ onye eme iphe,
dụ Chipfu ree.
24 Ụpfu -dụkpoo ya nụ;
ọ tọo darwudu alị;
kẹle ọo Chipfu gude ẹka
iya agbata iya ike.
25 Mu shikwa teke mu bụ nwata
nọwaa jasụ mu bụru
ọgerenya nta-a;
ọle mu teke ahụma-swee
ẹka Chipfu gbadoru
onye pfụberekoto;
ọzoo ẹka ụnwu iya
arwọ nri.
26 Onye pfụberekoto anọduje eme
iphe-ọma tekenteke; ọ nọdu
ejieje nemadzụ ụgwo.
Ee-shi l'ẹhu ụnwu iya kebe
ndu ọzo l'ọma.
27 Gbakụtaru eme ẹjo-ememe azụ;
watachia eme iphe, dụ ree;
g'iiburu l'alị-a jasụ l'ojejoje.
28 Chipfu bẹ ọ dụ ree
g'ụnwu-eliphe kpeje ikpe,
pfụru nhamụnha;
ọphu ọ hajẹdu ndu yeru iya
obu ẹka.
Ọokwa-chije phẹ akwachi
jasụ l'ojejoje;
obenu l'awa ndu ẹjo-iphe
bẹ oo-me g'ẹphe chịhu.
29 Ndu pfụberekoto e-mechaa
nweru alị-a;
buru iya jasụ lẹ tuutuutuu
lẹ mịimiimii.
30 Opfu, eshije onye pfụberekoto
l'ọnu fụta bụ mmamiphe.
Ire iya bụru iphe, adụje iya
nụ bụ epfu iphe,
pfụru ọto.
31 Iphe, adụje iya l'obu
tekenteke bụ ekemu
Chileke;
ọphu ọ haswejedu ọkpa
l'ememe iya.
32 Ndu ẹjo-iphe ejeje ewoha
onwophẹ kwabẹru ndu
pfụberekoto;
g'ee-shi g'ọ bụru ẹphe
-ghatashia;
ẹphe egbua phẹ.
33 Obenu lẹ Chipfu te ekwejedu
g'ẹphe daba phẹ l'ẹka;
ọphu a byadu anma ndu
pfụberekoto ikpe;
mẹ a kpụru phẹ bya
l'ụlo-ikpe.
34 Nọdukpoepho kwabẹru
Chipfu; tsoje ụzo iya!
Ọo ya e-kutse ngu mee
ngu l'i nworu alị-a.
Ii-gude ẹnya ngu hụma
teke ee-me gẹ ndu
ẹjo-iphe chịhu.
35 Mu hụmajewaru onye bụ
onye ẹjo-iphe.
Ọ dụkpoepho g'oshi,
ẹhu dụ guu, jaru dụ
kẹ kpuchi.
36 Obenu l'ọ tọ dụdu g'a
nọ-beru ọphu ọ dụedu.
Mu chọo ya nta chọo imo;
ọphu mu
ahụmaeduru iya;
37 Lenaa ndu ụta adụdu
l'ẹhu ẹnya;
mbụ-a; gbunaa ndu obu
phẹ gụru ìphóró igo;
hụma lẹ ndu eme gẹ nchị
dụ doo bẹ anọduje
eje l'iphu l'iphu.
38 Obenu lẹ ndu emeswe
Chipfu bẹ aa-tụkoepho
mebyishia.
Iphe, bụkpoo oshilọkpa
ndu ẹjo-iphe bẹ
ee-me g'ẹphe chịhu.
39 Ọo Chipfu adzọtaje ndu
pfụberekoto.
Ọo ya abụjeru agbarike phẹ
m'ẹphe nọdu eje
iphe-ẹhuka.
40 Mbụ lẹ Chipfu eyejeru phẹ
ẹka nafụta phẹ.
Ọonafutaje phẹ l'ẹka
ndu ẹjo-iphe;
shi nno dzọo phẹ;
kẹle ẹphe gbabarụ l'ime
iya je ezeru ndzụ.
