Epfupfu, Dévidi pfuru nụ Chileke
86
Ngabẹnu nchị; gụbe Chipfu;
zanụ mu ẹnu!
Kẹle mbẹdua bụ onye ụkpa;
bya abụru onye adụdu
g'ọ dụ iya.
2 Letanụ mu ẹnya;
kẹle mu yeru ngu obu.
Ọo ngu bụ Chileke mu;
dzọnaa mụbe onye-ozi ngu,
dakoberu ngu nụ.
3 Phụaru mu obu-imemini;
jiko Nnajịuphu;
kẹle mu eku ngu oku
mkpụrumkpuru.
4 Menaa g'ẹhu tsọo mụbe
onye-ozi ngu ụtso;
kẹle ọo gụbe Nnajịuphu bẹ
obu mu l'ophu araku.
5 Gụbe Nnajịuphu bụ onye ọma;
bya abụru onye agụjeru
nemadzụ nvụ.
Iphe, bụkpoo ndu arakuje
ngu nụ bẹ iiyeje obu
yegharụ iya ẹka.
6 Nụmanu epfupfu,
mu epfu anụ ngu
jiko Chipfu!
Ngabẹnu nchị nụma mkpu,
mu echiku ngu g'i meeru
mu eze-iphe-ọma!
7 Iphe -nọdu atsụ mu l'ẹhu bẹ
mu a-kpọ-kuje ngu;
kẹle mu maru l'ịi-nụma olu
mu.
8 Agwa, nọkota nụ ta adụdu
ọphu atụru gụbe Nnajịuphu;
ọphu ọ dụdu iphe, ẹphe eme,
dụru ọphu aatụ eyeru
ọphu gụbedua eme.
9 Ọhamoha ono,
i gude ẹka ngu mee ono
l'e-mechaa byakọta bya
abaarụ gụbe Nnajịuphu ẹja;
bya etua ngu ẹpha.
10 Noo kẹle ị parụ ẹka;
tẹme ị nọdu emeje iphe,
dụ biribiri.
Ọo ngu kpoloko kpẹekpu bẹ
bụ Chileke.
11 Jiko Chipfu; zinaa mu
ụzo ngu;
k'ọphu mu a-nọdu
etso ụzo ire-lanụ ngu ono.
Menaa g'ọriri mu ta agbashị
mu ụzo ọtu l'ẹka ị nọ;
k'ọphu mu a-nọdu
atsụ ẹpha ngu ebvu.
12 Mu gude obu mu g'ọ ha
ekele ngu ekele;
gụbe Nnajịuphu,
bụ Chileke mu.
Mu e-tu ngu ẹpha jasụ
l'ojejoje;
13 kẹle i yeru mu obu
yegharụ mu iya ẹka.
Noo kẹle ị nafụtaru ndzụ
mu l'alị-maa.
14 Oowaa Chileke! Ndu eku
onwophẹ wulihuakwaru
bya mu etso ọgu.
Tẹme ndu ewebushi akụrepho
dzukọbe g'ẹphe gbua mu;
mbụ ndu ono,
te eyeduru gụbe Chileke
ọnu ono.
15 Obenu lẹ gụbe Nnajịuphu
bụ Chileke,
aphụje obu-imemini;
bya abụru onye emeje
eze-iphe-ọma.
Ẹhu te eghujedu ngu eghu
ẹgwegwa;
tẹme ị nọdu eyeje obu
yegharụ iya ẹka;
bya abụru onye apfụshije
ike l'iphe, i pfuru.
16 Ghanaaru mu iphu;
meeru mu eze-iphe-ọma!
Wonụru ike ngu ye mụbe
onye-ozi ngu l'ẹhu!
Dzọnaa mụbe nwa,
nwanyị ono,
ejeru ngu ozi ono nwụru!
17 Goshinu mu iphe-ọhubama,
e-me gẹ mu maru l'ị
bụ onye ọma;
k'ọphu ndu ọhogu mu
a-hụma iya; iphere egude phẹ;
noo kẹle gụbe Chipfu
gbaru mu mkpu bya
adụa mu obu.
