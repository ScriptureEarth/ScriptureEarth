Ebvu-ọma, e deru onye-ishi ebvu; g'a kpọru une ye iya
67
Gẹ Chileke meeru anyi
eze-iphe-ọma;
gọru ọnu-ọma nụ anyi;
mekwaphọ g'iphu tụsahu
iya l'ẹhu anyi.
2 K'ọphu ụnwu-eliphe a-maru
ụzo ngu;
tẹme ọhamoha
amakwarụpho
l'ịidzotaje nemadzụ.
3 G'ụnwu-eliphe kelenụ gụbe
Chileke ekele!
G'iphe, bụkpoo nemadzụ
mgburugburu kele
ngu ekele!
4 G'ẹhu tsọnaa ọhamoha ụtso;
g'ẹphe tụnaa ụzu
ẹhu-ụtso!
Kẹle ndiphe l'ophu bẹ iikpeje
ikpe, pfụru ọto;
tẹme ọ bụru ngu anọduje
akpọ-ziru ọhamoha,
nọ l'eliphe ụzo.
5 G'ụnwu-eliphe kelekwa gụbe
Chileke ekele!
G'iphe, bụkpoo nemadzụ
mgburugburu kele
ngu ekele!
6 Ọo ya bụ; g'iphe, a kọberu l'alị
emehukwanụ emehu.
Tẹme Nnajịuphu, bụ Chileke
anyi ono agọru ọnu-ọma
nụ anyi.
7 Chileke a-gọru ọnu-ọma nkiya
ono nụ anyi.
Ọo ya bụ gẹ ndiphe l'ophu
jasụ l'ẹka igwe beru
alị beru awatakwanụ
iya atsụ ebvu!
