Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu; g'a kpọru une ye iya
4
Zaa mu ẹnu mẹ mu raku ngu;
jiko Chileke mu; onye
pfụberekoto.
Ị gbaru mu mkpu teke mu shi
eje iphe-ẹhuka.
Jiko phụaru mu obu-imemini;
nụmaro epfupfu,
mu epfu anụ ngu!
2 ?Bụ teke ole bẹ unubẹ
ụnwu-eliphe a-ha egude ùbvù,
a kwabẹru mu eme iberezi?
?Bụ teke ole bẹ unu e-yeberu
obu l'iphe, enwedu ishi;
mbụ woru onwunu haarụ
iphe, abụdu ọkpobe iphe?
3 Ọle ọphu unu a-maru bụ lẹ
Chipfu họtawaru ndu
yeru iya obu doberu
onwiya.
Mu -chiku Chipfu bẹ ọozaje
mu ẹnu.
4 Ẹhu -ghuahaa unu eghu;
unu te ekwekwa
g'ẹhu-eghu ọbu kpatarụ
unu eme iphe-ẹji.
Unu zẹkpoepho nwapyakọo
l'iphe, unu azẹje azẹe;
tọo nwẹhu rịa ya arịri;
nọ-kirishia.
5 Gẹ ngwẹja, unu a-nọdu egwe
anụ Chileke bụkwaru
ngwẹja, dụ iya ree.
Unu adakobeje Chipfu.
6 Ikpoto nemadzụ asụje:
“?Jiko Chipfu; mebanaaru
anyi iphe ọma.
Menaa g'iphe dụru anyi lẹ
ree!”
7 Obenu l'ẹhu-ụtso ọphu i meru;
ọ nọdu atsọ mbẹdua
bẹ kakwa ọphu atsọje
phẹ nụ;
mẹ iphe, ẹphe meberu
l'opfu phẹ mehu shingushingu.
8 Mu a-tọkpoepho nwẹhu
zẹ-zeta kuru mgbẹnya
l'ẹhu-guu;
kẹle ọo gụbe Chipfu kpoloko
emeje gẹ mu maru l'ọ tọ
dụdu iphe, sụru mu emebata.
