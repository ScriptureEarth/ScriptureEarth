Ebvu-ọma, Asafu deru
79
Gụbe Chileke; hụmanu lẹ ndu
ọhozo sọ-kputewaru
alị ono,
bụ okiphe ngu ono.
Ẹphe pharwụshiwaru eze-ụlo
ngu ono, dụ nsọ ono;
bya emewaa Jierúsalẹmu;
ọ bụru ikpọzu.
2 Odzu ndu ejeru ngu ozi
bẹ ẹphe wowaru
tọgbogbaaru ẹnu,
ephe l'eli g'o ria;
mbụ l'odzu ndu dụru ngu
nsọ bẹ ẹphe wowaru
tụaru anụ-ẹgbudu
nanụ nanụ.
3 Jierúsalẹmu l'ophu gba
mgburugburu bẹ
ẹphe gbashịru
mee nemadzụ gẹ mini;
ọphu ọ dụedu onye
nọkwadu nụ, e-gburu
ẹja tukobe ndu ono,
wụshiru ẹgube ono.
4 Ndu obutobu anyi phẹ mẹ
ndu bupheru anyi
mgburugburu
tụko ekweru anyi une bya
achị anyi ọchi.
5 ?Bụ teke ole bẹ ẹhu
e-ghuberu ngu eghu
gụbe Chipfu?
?Ẹhu e-ghuwa ngu rọ eghu
jasụ l'ojejoje tọo?
?Bụ teke ole bẹ okophoo
ngu e-nwuberu
phuruphuru g'ọku
kparụ?
6 Wonụru ẹhu-eghu ngu
tukoshi ndu ọhozo ono,
amadụ ngu nụ ono!
Tukoshikwa iya phọ alị-eze
ono,
ete ekujedu ẹpha ngu ono!
7 Kẹle ẹphe gbuwaru oshilọkpa
Jiékọpu pyaapyaa;
bya emebyishiwa unuphu
ibe phẹ.
8 Te emekwa g'anyi nwụa
ọnwu iphe-ẹji,
nna anyi oche phẹ meru!
Phụaru anyi obu-imemini
ẹgwegwa;
kẹle anyi dabyiwaru
mgbegere.
9 Yeru anyi ẹka jiko Chileke;
mbụ gụbedua,
bụ onye ndzọta anyi;
k'ọphu ee-shi nno kwabẹ
ẹpha ngu ùbvù.
Gụaru anyi nvụ l'iphe-ẹji anyi;
nafụta anyi;
g'ẹpha ngu apfụru!
10 ?Dẹnu g'o gude ndu ọhozo
je a-nọduje ajị-phe sụ:
“?Dẹnu ẹka Chileke
phẹ nọ?”
Nọdunu l'iphu anyi mee gẹ
ndu ọhozo ono madzuru
l'ị gwataru ụgwo ọchi
ndu ejeru ngu ozi ono,
e gbushiru ono.
11 G'ude, ndu nọ l'ọka mkpọro
atsụ rwunaa ngu nchị!
Gudenụ ọkpehu ngu mee gẹ
ndu e kperu mpfugbu
nweru onwophẹ.
12 Pfụa obutobu anyi phẹ ụgwo
iphe,
ẹphe meru ngu ugbo ẹsaa;
mbụ iphe-iphere,
ẹphe mekọtaru kpua
gụbe Chipfu.
13 Ọo ya bụ g'anyịbe ndibe ngu
etua ngu ẹpha jeye
l'ojejoje;
mbụ anyịbe atụru,
iiche echeche ono;
mbụ l'anyi e-tu ngu ẹpha;
e -shi l'ọgbo
sweru ọgbo.
