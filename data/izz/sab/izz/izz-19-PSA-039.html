Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu, bụ Jiedutunu
39
Mu sụru lẹ mu a-kwabẹru
ụzo mu ẹnya;
gẹ mu te egude ọnu mu
pfuta ẹjo-iphe.
Ọnu mu bẹ mu akụje ngga;
mẹ ndu ẹjo-iphe
nọ-kube mu.
2 Mu woru ẹka hua l'ọnu
nọ-kirishia;
gẹ mu te epfukpọnu g'ọ ka
mma l'ọo iphe, dụ ree.
Ọle mgburwashị-ẹhu mu
akaẹle-a shii eje.
3 A nọnyaa; obu wahụahaa mu
awahụ;
mbụ mu nọdu rịnyae ya
phọ;
ọ gbanwuhu mu
l'ẹhu g'ọku.
Mu sanaa ọnu wata epfu opfu sụ:
4 Gụbe Chipfu; menaa mu
gẹ mu maru gẹ
mu a-nọ-beru;
gẹ mbọku e-beru mu
anwụhu!
Menaa gẹ mu maru gẹ ndzụ
mu dụ-be mkpirikpu.
5 I meru ndzụ mu; ọ dụ tsọkere.
Gẹ ndzụ mu a-nọ-beru ta
adụdu iphe,
ọ bụ l'iphu ngu.
Nemadzụ l'ophu tụkoerupho
bụru udzu ọnu.
6 Nemadzụ g'ọogha-phe nno
bụepho phẹrephere
mmanụ bẹ ọ bụ.
Kpanga kpanga kpanga,
ọonoduje eme bụepho
g'ọ bụru.
Ọokpaje ẹku kụberu onwiya;
ọle ọ tọ makwanụ onye
e-mechaa ria ya nụ.
7 Nta-a Chipfu; ?bụkpoo gụnu
bẹ mu ele ẹnya iya?
Ọ kwa ngu bẹ mu daru
ngabẹru.
8 Nafụta mu l'emeswe ono,
mu mesweru ngu ono!
Be mekwa mu gẹ mu bụru
onye ndu eswe
e-kweru une!
9 Mu shi woru ẹka hua
l'ọnu nọ-kirishia;
noo kẹle ọo gụbedua bẹ
iphe ono, anwụkotaru
mu nụ ono shi l'ẹka.
10 Hanaa mu echi ẹchachi-a,
iichi mu-a;
kẹle ẹka iichi mu gbaa
gbaa bẹ ike abvụakwa
mu ree.
11 Ị -baarụ nemadzụ mba gude
goshi iya ẹjo-iphe,
oome
bẹ bụepho gẹ nsụ,
asụkashije iphe-a bụ
g'ịi-nọduje asụkashi iphe,
dụ ree, onye ono
nweru.
Nemadzụ g'ẹphe ha
bụkota udzu ọnu bẹ ẹphe bụ.
12 “Nụmanu epfupfu,
mu epfu anụ ngu
gụbe Chipfu!
Ngabẹnu nchị lẹ mkpu,
mu echiku ngu!
Ba gbakwa nkụchi g'ị tị
nụma ẹkwa, mu ara
ekpu ngu!
Kẹle mu bụ onye lwarụ
alwalwa, lwapfutaru
ngu nụ;
ẹgube ono, ndiche
anyi phẹ shikwaphọ
bụru ono.
13 Jiko hafụa mu rọ; k'ọphu
ẹhu a-kabẹ tsọ mu
ụtso ọzo;
tẹme mu alashịwaro maa.”
