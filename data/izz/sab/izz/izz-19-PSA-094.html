94
Gụbe Chipfu, bụ Chileke,
bụ onye agwatajẹ ụgwo iphe;
jiko wanaa g'ẹnyanwu
g'a hụma ngu!
2 Gbalihunu gụbe onye ekpeje
mgboko l'ophu ikpe!
Jiko pfụnaa ndu eku onwophẹ
ụgwo, gbaru phẹ nụ!
3 Gụbe Chipfu; ?ndu ẹjo-iphe
a-gbọkpo kwakoko-a,
ẹphe agbọ-a jasụ teke ole?
4 Ndu eme ẹjo-ememe
anọduje etu onwophẹ
ọphushugbu.
Ẹphe epfunụkaje okporokpo
opfu,
ọnu phẹ asụdu epfupfu.
5 Ndibe gụbe Chipfu bẹ ẹphe
egweje uji; mbụ ndu ono,
bụ okiphe ngu ono bẹ ẹphe
akpapyashị ishi.
6 Ụnwanyi, ji phẹ nwụhuru;
yẹe ndu lwarụ alwalwa
bẹ ẹphe aphụje aphụphu.
Ẹphe eworu ndu enwedu
nna gbushia.
7 Ọobujeru; ẹphe -nọdu;
ẹphe asụ: “Chipfu ta adụkwa
iphe, ọohumaje;
mbụ lẹ Chileke kẹ Jiékọpu
ta adụkwa iphe,
ọokpa ishi iya.”
8 G'ọwa-a dokwaa unu ẹnya;
unubẹ ndu egomunggo
swehuru l'echilabọ
ụnwu-eliphe; mbụ-a;
unubẹ ndu eme eswe;
?bụ teke ole bẹ
unu a-makpọoru iphe?
9 ?Bụ onye ono, bụ iya
pfuberu nemadzụ nchị
ta anụdu iphe
lẹ nchị?
Tọo ?bụ onye ono,
moberu nemadzụ ẹnya
ta aphụdu ụzo l'onwiya?
10 ?Bụchia onye ono,
agbẹje ahụ̀ ndu amadụ
iya nụ ahụ̀hù ono
te edobedu unu g'unu
a-dụ;
mbụ ?bụ yẹbedua ono,
anọduje ezi nemadzụ
mmamiphe gbẹchia ọphu
ọ madụ iphe l'onwiya?
11 Chipfu makọtaru iphe,
nemadzụ anọduje arị.
Ọ maru l'ọriri phẹ bụkota
iphe-mmanụ.
12 Sụ-a; ẹhu-ụtso bẹ a gọru
nụ onye gụbe Chipfu
ahụ̀je ahụ̀hù;
mbụ onye iigudeje ekemu
ngu ezi iphe.
13 Onye ono bẹ iimeje gẹ
meji nmapfuru m'ọ
nọdu eje iphe-ẹhuka;
jasụ mbọku,
ee-bu nsụ gẹ ndu ẹjo-iphe
wụlahu.
14 Noo kẹle Chipfu ta
abyakpọdaa aha ndibe
iya;
ọphu ọ byakpodaa ekwe gẹ
ndu bụ okiphe iya laa l'iswi.
15 Noo kẹle ndu-ikpe a-wata
ekpe ikpe, pfụru ọto.
Ọ bụerupho ndu obu phẹ gụru
ìphóró bụ ndu a-dụ ike
tsokọta ikpe ono.
16 ?Bụ onye a-gbẹshi pfụ-doru
mu;
mekpeeru mu ndu
ẹjo-iphe?
?Bụ onye a-pfụru l'ẹkiya
nkemu lwụ-kpeeru mu ndu
eme ẹjo-ememe?
17 Ọ tọ bụ lẹ Chipfu gbaru
mu mkpu;
mẹ mu anọduhawaro l'alị
ọ-larụ-nọdu-nggujingguji
teke dụ ẹnya.
18 Teke mu chiru mkpu sụ
l'ẹpurukutu echikọtaakwa
mu bẹ n-yemobu nkengu
wọtaru mu gẹ mu ta ada.
19 Teke mu watarụ arị oke ọriri
bẹ ị dụru mu obu mee;
ẹhu tsọahaa mu ụtso.
20 ?Ii-kwe gẹ gụ lẹ ndu-ishi,
ete emedu iphe,
pfụru ọto megba iphe;
mbụ ndu egudeje ekemu
emekpa ndu ẹphe achị ẹhu?
21 Ẹphe edzukobeje g'ẹphe
kpapyaa ndu
pfụberekoto ishi.
Ẹphe woru onye adụdu iphe,
o meru kpee mpfugbu.
22 Obenu l'ọo Chipfu bụ onye
egbobutaje mu nụ.
Ọo Chileke mu bụ oke
agbara-mkpuma ono,
mu agbabajẹ je
edomia onwomu ono.
23 Ọo ya a-pfụ phẹ ụgwo iphe-ẹji,
ẹphe eme;
tẹme ọ bụru iya e-mebyishi
phẹ k'ẹjo-iphe ono,
ẹphe eme ono.
Ọo Chipfu, bụ Chileke anyi
e-mebyishi phẹ.
