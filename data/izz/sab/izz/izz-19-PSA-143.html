Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru
143
Gụbe Chipfu;
nụmanu epfupfu, mu epfu
anụ ngu.
Ngabẹnu nchị nụma mkpu,
mu echi sụ g'i meeru
mu eze-iphe-ọma.
Eshinu ị bụ onye pfụberekoto
bya abụru onye apfụshije
ike l'iphe, i pfuru;
jiko zanụ mu ẹnu!
2 Jiko te ekpeshinu mụbe
onye-ozi ngu ikpe;
kele ọ tọ dụdu nemadzụ,
dzụ ndzụ, pfụberekoto
l'iphu ngu.
3 Onye ọhogu mu chịru
mu gude;
bya echita mu tụa l'alị;
bya emee mu;
mu nọdu l'ẹka gbakọtaru
ọchii g'akahụ maa,
nwụhuru lẹ ndiche.
4 Noo g'o gude ike wata
mu abvụbvu.
Obu wata mu akụ photophoto.
5 Mu nyata g'o shi adụje
teke ndiche.
Mu bya anọdu nggujingguji
rịahaa iphe, bụkpoo iphe,
i mekọtaru anyi;
bya elerwebaa ẹnya ree
l'iphemiphe,
i gude ẹka ngu mee.
6 Mu chiliaru ngu ẹka mu labọ
imeli.
Ẹgu ngu gụahaa obu mu
g'ẹgu-mini anọduje agụ
alị-ọkponku-a.
7 Zanụ mu ẹnu ẹgwegwa jiko
Chipfu;
kẹle ike abvụwaa mu l'ele
ẹnya ngu.
Be domikwaru mu iphu
ngu edomi;
ọdumeka bẹ mu a-dụkwa
gẹ ndu e liru l'ilu.
8 O -rwutashịa echele gẹ
mu nụmanu opfu,
a-nọdu akọru mu kẹ
n-yemobu ngu;
kẹle ọo ngu bẹ mu dakoberu.
Jiko goshinụ mu ụzo,
mu e-shije;
kẹle ọo l'ẹka ị nọ bẹ obu
mu nọ.
9 Nafụtanu mu l'ẹka ndu ọhogu
mu phẹ jiko Chipfu;
kẹle ọo l'ime ngu bẹ mu
domiru onwomu.
10 Zia mu iphe, bụ uche ngu
gẹ mu meje;
kẹle ọo ngu bụ Chileke mu.
Jiko gẹ Unme nkengu,
bụ ọkpobe Unme ono
dunaa mu shia ụzo,
pfụru nhamụnha.
11 Kebenu ndzụ mu l'ọma jiko
Chipfu; g'ẹpha ngu apfụru.
Gudenụ apfụbekoto
nkengu nafụta mu
l'iphe-ẹhuka-a, mu eje-a.
12 Gudenụ kẹle i yeru mu obu;
mee g'ọnu bụru ndu ọhogu
mu kpụrukudukuu.
L'ị tụko ndu achị mu lẹ njọ
g'ẹphe ha mee g'ẹphe
bụru mkpurupyata;
noo kẹle mu bụ
onye ejeru ngu ozi.
