Ẹ́vù, a kwarụ nụ onye-ishi ebvu g'ụnwu Kora gụa; g'e gude olu ebvu, “Ojimbvu” gụa ya; ebvu ekele-nwanyị
45
Obu mu bẹ opfu ọma jiru kẹ
pyịmu pyịmu.
Onye mu epfu eyeru bụkwa
eze anyi.
Ire mu dụwaa gẹ
mkpara-ẹkwo onye maru
ede ẹkwo ẹgwegwa.
2 Ị kakọta nemadzụ,
a nwụkpooru anwụnwu
ama mma.
Eze-iphe-ọma jiru ngu ọnu ejiji;
kẹle Chileke gọru ọnu-ọma
ojejoje nụ ngu.
3 Gụbe O-gbua-o-turu-ugo;
wota ogu-echi ngu kee
l'upfu!
Gbaphua onwongu akpabiri
yẹe o-gbu-nwịinwii!
4 Dụnu akpabiri akpabiri
nyikoru l'ụgbo-ịnya ngu
gbaru kwaakwaakwaa
je alwụa lwụ-kpee!
See iswi ire-lanụ;
yẹe ewoze onwonye alị;
yẹe apfụbekoto.
G'ẹka-ụtara ngu mekwaa iphe,
dụ ebvu.
5 G'apfụ ngu, atsụ nkọ
mejeẹpho tsụrumu l'obu
ndu ọhogu gụbe eze.
G'ọhamoha dajẹkwa
kpurumu l'iphu ngu.
6 Gụbe Chileke; ọo g'ị bụ eze
nta-a bụ g'ịi-bụru iya jasụ
lẹ tuutuutuu lẹ mịimiimii.
Ikpe, pfụru ọto bẹ i gude
bụru eze; l'ẹka ị bụ eze.
7 Iphe, pfụru ọto dụ ngu ree;
ẹjo-iphe dụkwanu ngu
ashị.
Noo g'o gude Chileke,
bụ Chileke ngu họta ngu
l'echilabọ ndu unu l'ẹphe
bụ ọgbo; bya awụa ngu
manụ ẹhu-ụtso l'ishi.
8 Uwe ngu g'ọ ha bẹ mkpọ
mẹru; waa alowe;
yẹe kashia tụko vọo
pfurupfuru.
Une, a -gbẹ l'iphe, bụkpoo
unuphu ibe gụbe eze
akpọgbaa nọdu emeje
g'ẹhu tsọo ngu ụtso;
mbụ unuphu ọphu e
gudegbaa eze-enyi
mematagbaa.
9 Ụnwada eze hachakwaaru
yịru l'ụnwanyi aakwabẹ
ùbvù l'ibe ngu.
Ọo l'ẹkutara ngu bẹ
eze-nwanyị pfụru.
Iphe, o yeru l'ẹhu bụru iphe,
e meru lẹ mkpọla-ododo
Ofi.
10 Ngabẹ nchị gụbe ada-eze!
Jiko rịkwaa iphe-a,
mu abya epfupfu-a arịri!
Zọhakota ndibe unu;
waa ndu nna ngu phẹ!
11 Lẹ mma, ịima meakwaru;
ị nọdu agụ eze ẹgu.
Eshinu ọ bụ iya bụ nnajịuphu
ngu; phozejekwaru iya!
12 Ndu Taya a-nụkwa ngu iphe
kẹ mmanụ;
tẹme ndu nweru iphe
emeahaaru ngu ree;
g'eeshi g'ẹphe tụba ngu
l'ẹnya.
13 Ada eze bẹ nọ
l'o-gbu-nwịinwii iya;
e duba iya l'ụlo.
Uwe iya bẹ e gude oghu
mkpọla-ododo kwee.
14 E yeru iya uwe, e meru
ẹrengete duru ejepfu eze.
Ụnwumgboko ibe iya nọdu
etso iya l'azụ.
Ẹphebedua bẹ eedukwaphọ
edujeru eze.
15 Ẹhu atsọ phẹ ụtso;
ẹphe nọdu ete ẹswa;
e duru phẹ abahụ l'ibe eze.
16 Gụbe eze; ọo ụnwu ngu
bẹ a-nọchi ẹnya
ochochoroche ngu phẹ
bụru eze.
Ọ bụru phẹ bẹ ii-me g'ẹphe
bụru ishi lẹ mgboko
mgburugburu.
17 Mu e-me g'a nyatajẹ ngu;
e -shi l'ọgbo sweru ọgbo.
Ọo ya bụ g'ọhamoha
ekeleje ngu ekele jasụ lẹ
tuutuutuu lẹ mịimiimii.
