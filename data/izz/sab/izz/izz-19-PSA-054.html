Ẹ́vù, Dévidi kwarụ nụ onye-ishi ebvu; g'a kpọru une ye iya
Teke ọ kwarụ iya bụ gẹ ndu Zifu byaru bya asụ Sọlu lẹ Dévidi wohakwaru onwiya l'echilabọ phẹ
54
Gudenụ ẹpha ngu dzọo mu;
gụbe Chileke!
Gudenụ ọkpehu ngu haarụ
mu enge!
2 Nụmanu epfupfu, mu epfu
anụ ngu; jiko Chileke.
Ngabẹnu nchị; nụma iphe,
shi mu l'ọnu.
3 Kẹle ndu shi eshishi wụru
bya mu etso opfu;
Ndu ewebushi akụrepho nọdu
achọ mu egbugbu;
mbụ ndu ata akpadu
Chileke ishi m'ilile.
4 Obenu l'ọo Chileke bụ onye
eyejeru mu ẹka.
Ọo Nnajịuphu bụ onye gude
ndzụ mu.
5 Gẹ Chileke mekwaa
g'ẹjo-iphe, ndu achị mu
lẹ njọ meru mu
laphuru phẹ azụ!
Gẹ Chileke mebyishikwaa
phẹ gude nno goshi
l'ọopfushije ike l'iphe,
o pfuru.
6 Mu a-tụ obu mu onyo
gwetarụ ngu ngwẹja.
Mu abya ekele ngu ekele
gụbe Chipfu;
kẹle ẹpha ngu dụ ree.
7 Tẹme ọ bụru ngu
dzọtakotaru mu l'iphe,
bụkpoo iphe-ẹhuka,
byapfutaru mu nụ.
Mu byakwaphọ ahụma l'e
mekperu ndu ọhogu
mu phẹ.
