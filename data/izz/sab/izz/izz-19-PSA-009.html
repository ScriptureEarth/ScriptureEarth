Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu; g'e gude olu ebvu, “Ẹkwa Nwa” gụa ya
9
Gụbe Chipfu; mu e-gude obu
mu l'ophu ekele ngu
ekele.
Mu a-nọdu epfukashị
iphe, dụ biribiri,
iimeshigbaa.
2 Ẹhu a-nọdu atsọ mu ụtso;
mu egude ngu ete ẹswa.
Mu agụ ebvu etu ngu ẹpha;
gụbe
Ọkalibe-kangokọtaru-nụ!
3 Ndu ọhogu mu tsuphuwaru
azụ!
Ẹphe vukotawaru iphe daa;
bụru mkpurupyata
l'atatiphu ngu!
4 Noo kẹle i kpewaru ikpe,
pfụru ọto bya ahaaru
mu enge.
Ị nọ l'aba-eze ngu kpee ikpe,
pfụru nhamụnha.
5 Ị bawaru ọhamoha mba;
bya emee ndu ẹjo-iphe;
ẹphe bụru mkpurupyata.
I mewaru g'ẹpha phẹ
chịhu achịhu jasụ
lẹ tuutuutuu lẹ
mịimiimii.
6 Ndu ọhogu mu phẹ bẹ
nkephẹ bvụwaru.
Ibe phẹ a-bụerupho ochobu
jasụru asụru.
Mkpụkpu phẹ bẹ ị vụwaru
l'ọgbarabvu;
ọphu ọ dụedu onye
nyatarụ iya nụ.
7 Chipfu a-bụru eze jasụ
lẹ tuutuutuu lẹ
mịimiimii.
O mewaru aba-eze iya ono
g'ọ ngụru angụru;
k'ọphu ọo-nọdu iya kpee
ikpe.
8 Oo-kpe ndu mgboko ikpe,
pfụru ọto.
Ikpe, ookpeje apfụjeru
nhamụnha.
9 Ndu eemekpa ẹhu agbabajẹ
Chipfu l'ẹka je ezeeru
ndzụ.
Ndu iphe-ẹhuka byaru
agbabajẹkwa iya
phọ l'ẹka je edomia
onwophẹ.
10 Iphe, bụkpoo ndu maru ngu
nụ bụ ngu bẹ ẹphe
dakoberu;
kẹle gụbe Chipfu teke
agbadoswee ndu achọ
ngu amaru.
11 Unu gụa ebvu gude tua
Chipfu ono, bụ eze lẹ
Zayọnu ono ẹpha.
Unu tụaru ọhamoha ọnu iphe,
o meru.
12 Noo kẹle yẹbedua, akpajẹ
ishi ọchi onye e gburu
egbugbu;
bẹ nyatakwarụ phẹ nụ.
Ndu iphe-ẹhuka byaru -chia
mkpu bẹ ọ tọo
gbajẹkwa phẹ nkụchi.
13 Gụbe Chipfu; phụaru mu
obu-imemini!
Lewarọ gẹ ndu achị mu lẹ njọ
akpabe mu ẹhu!
Lọfutanu mu l'ọnu-ọguzo maa!
14 Ọo ya bụ gẹ mu anọdu
l'ọnu-abata Nwada Zayọnu
ono jaa ngu ajaja;
bya etekwaaphọ ẹswa l'ị
dzọtaru mu.
15 Ọhamoha dalahụwaru l'iduma,
ẹphe buru nwẹka phẹ.
Ẹphe jewaru ekoda l'ụgbu,
ẹphe geru l'onwophẹ.
16 Iphe, e gude maru Chipfu bụ
g'oogudeje ekpe ikpe.
Ndu ẹjo-iphe egudeje
ẹjo-ememe, ẹphe eme
gbabẹru onwophẹ
ọ́nyà dalahụ.
17 Ndu ẹjo-iphe bẹ aa-chịru ye
l'alị-maa;
mbụ l'iphe, bụkpoo ọha,
zọharu Chileke bẹ
a-lakọta l'alị-maa.
18 Obenu lẹ ndu adụdu g'ọ
dụ phẹ bẹ ọ tọo
zọhajekwa jasụru;
ọphu iphe, ndu ụkpa
ele ẹnya lẹ Chipfu
e-meru phẹ
abyadụ ala kẹ mmanụ.
19 Gbalihukpọdapho gụbe Chipfu;
g'ọ tọ bụ nemadzụ
l'agbẹ ka ngu ike!
G'e kpee ọhamoha ikpe l'iphu
ngu!
20 Chipfu; menaa gẹ
ndzụ-agụgu rwuta phẹ;
k'ọphu ọhamoha ono
a-maru l'ọo nemadzụ
mmanụ bẹ ẹphe bụ.
