114
Teke ndu Ízurẹlu shi lẹ
Ijiputu fụta;
mbụ teke oshilọkpa
Jiékọpu shi l'ẹka ndu
opfu phẹ eswe uswe lẹ
nchị fụta;
2 bẹ Jiuda byaru bya abụru
onye dụru Chileke nsọ.
Ndu Ízurẹlu bya abụru ndu o
nwe enwenwe.
3 Eze Ẹnyimu hụma iya bya
eye ọkpa l'ọso;
tẹme ẹnyimu Jiọ́danu
dakọbe lashịa azụ.
4 Oke úbvú nọdu apfụgba kẹ
shọoshoo g'ebili.
Úbvú nọdu apfụ kẹ
karagidingu karagidingu
gẹ nwatụru.
5 Sụ-a; gụbe Eze Ẹnyimu;
?ị gbaru gụnu?
Gụbe Jiọ́danu;
?bụ ole bụ k'ala azụ?
6 Unubẹ oke úbvú;
?bụ gụnu bẹ unu gude
apfụgba kẹ shọoshoo g'ebili?
Unubẹ úbvú;
?bụhunu ole bẹ unu gude
apfụ kẹ karagidingu
karagidingu gẹ nwatụru?
7 Eliphe l'ophu nmakwaa jiijiijii
l'iphu Nnajịuphu;
mbụ l'iphu Chileke
kẹ Jiékọpu.
8 Onye gbazeru mkpuma;
ọ bụru okpuru;
bya eworu alị,
dụ epfu epfu mee;
ọ bụru ishi nggele.
