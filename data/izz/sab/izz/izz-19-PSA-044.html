Ẹ́vù, a kwarụ nụ onye-ishi ebvu g'ụnwu Kora gụa
44
Gụbe Chileke; anyi gudekwa
nchị anyi nụma;
tẹme nna anyi oche phẹ
kọkwaaru iya phọ anyi;
mbụ iphemiphe ono,
i mekọtaru phẹ teke
ndiche ono.
2 L'ọo gụbedua l'onwongu
chịshiru ndu ọhozo l'alị phẹ;
bya eworu ẹphebedua
dzabẹ l'alị ono.
Lẹ ndu dụ igwerigwe bẹ i
mebyishiru;
obenu lẹ nna anyi oche
phẹ bẹ i meru g'ẹphe
jaa àjàjà.
3 Ọ tọ bụdu ogu-echi phẹ bẹ
ẹphe gude lwụta alị ono;
tẹme ọphu ọ bụdu
ọkpehu phẹ bẹ ẹphe gude
lwụ-kpee.
Obenu l'ọo ẹkutara ngu;
yẹe ọkpehu nkengu;
waa anọ-kube ono,
ị nọ-kube phẹ ono bẹ meru;
ẹphe lwụ-kpee;
noo kẹle ẹphe tụbaru ngu
l'ẹnya.
4 Ọo gụbedua bụ Chileke mu;
bya abụru onye
eze nkemu;
mbụ onye emeje g'oshilọkpa
Jiékọpu lwụ-kpee l'ọgu.
5 Ọo l'ẹka ngu bẹ anyi anọduje
nwuta ndu achị anyi lẹ
njọ nwuphu azụ.
Ọ bụru ẹpha ngu bẹ anyi
egudeje zọo ndu
wulihuru etso anyi
opfu pyakapyaka.
6 Mu ta chịduru ụpfu kwẹe
l'apfụ, mu chị;
tẹme mu maru l'ogu-echi
mu ta adzọdu mu.
7 Obenu l'ọo ike nkengu bẹ
anyi gude lwụ-kpee
ndu achị anyi lẹ njọ.
Ndu anyi dụ ashị bẹ i
meru iphere kpua.
8 Ọ kwa Chileke bẹ anyi
egudeje eku onwanyi
mbọku-mbọku.
Ọ bụru gụbe Chileke bẹ anyi
e-keleje ekele jasụ lẹ
tuutuutuu lẹ mịimiimii.
9 Ọle ị jịkaru anyi;
bya ekpua anyi iphu lẹ ntụ;
ọphu iitsojeẹduru ndu
ojọgu anyi eje ọgu.
10 Ị harụ ndu ọhogu anyi;
ẹphe chiphu anyi azụ;
ndu anyi dụ ashị bya atụko
ẹku anyi kwaa ọkwata.
11 Ị gbadoru anyi g'atụru,
e doberu k'egbushi.
Ị chịa anyi; anyi daa ịghagha
l'ọhamoha ọzo.
12 I wowaru ndibe ngu reshia
ọ-dụ-l'urwu;
k'ọphu bụ l'ọ tọ dụdu urwu,
i ritaru l'ishi phẹ.
13 I meru; ndu obutobu anyi
nọdu ekweru anyi une.
Ndu nọ-pheru anyi
mgburugburu nọdu
anọduje epfubyishi anyi;
bya achị anyi ọchi.
14 I meru; anyi bụru iphe,
ndu ọhozo gude anmaku
l'ẹjo ẹtu;
bụru ndu bụ: a -nyata
phẹ l'e phuphee ishi.
15 Mbọku-mbọku bẹ aanọduje
eme mu iphe-iphere
mmekwasẹ.
Iphere nọdu ejije mu iphu
pyịmu;
16 noo kẹle ndu ọhogu mu;
waa ndu eme g'ẹphe
gwata mu ụgwo-iphe
anọduje ekweru mu une;
epfutọshi mu.
17 Iphemiphe-a mekọtaru
l'ẹhu anyi; ọle anyi ta
zọhaduru ngu;
ọphu anyi emebyiduru
ọgbandzu anyi lẹ ngu.
18 Ọphu obu anyi alụfuduru
l'ẹka ị nọ;
ọphu anyi ahataduru ọkpa
zọbe k'eshiswe ụzo ngu.
19 Obenu l'i gwerụ anyi
arakabya l'ẹgu nkụta-ọswa;
bya eworu dadishi-nwamaa
phukpute anyi.
20 Ọme anyi zọharu Chileke anyi;
bya achị-liaru agwa ndu
ọhozo ẹka;
21 ?Chileke tege avọdu iya
vọfuta tọo;
eshinu ọ makọtaru iphe,
e domiru edomi,
dụ nemadzụ l'obu.
22 Ọle ọ bụ; ọo l'iswi ẹhu ngu
bẹ ọ tọ dụdu teke ọnwu
ta anọdujedu eme g'o
gbua anyi;
tẹme l'eeme anyi g'atụru,
a chịtaru eje egbushi.
23 Gụbe Nnajịuphu; tehu etehu!
?Dẹnu g'o gude i je anọdu
eku mgbẹnya?
Tehu etehu; ba ajịkashi anyi
ojejoje!
24 ?Dẹnu g'o gude ọphu i
yeheduru anyi ọnu?
?Dẹnu g'o gude ị zọhaa
iphe-ẹhuka, anyi eje;
bya azọhaa l'aakpa anyi ẹhu?
25 E mewaru anyi; anyi daa;
nmaa ẹpho l'alị;
k'ọphu bụ l'ishi tsụtawaru
anyi ẹja.
26 Gbalihu yeru anyi ẹka!
Guderọ n-yemobu ngu
gbafụta anyi!
