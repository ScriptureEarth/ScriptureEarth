Ọshi, ayọru ndu kpọru obu yeru ndu Ijiputu
31
Nshịo unubẹ ndu ono, agba ala alị ndu Ijiputu eje achọ n-yemẹka; ndu daphuberu ịnya; bya akpọru obu yeru ikpoto ụgbo-ịnya, ẹphe nweru; yẹe oke ọkpehu dụ ndu ojọgu phẹ, bụ ndu egudeje ịnya eje ọgu; ọphu ẹphe adabẹduru ẹnya l'ẹka Onye nsọ kẹ ndu Ízurẹlu nọ; mbụ ọphu ẹphe agbapfuduru Chipfu g'o yeru phẹ ẹka. 2 Ọle ọ bụ; yẹbedua l'onwiya bụkwaa onye maru iphe. Ọ nọdu ewotajeru nemadzụ ọla-l'iswi; ọphu oopfujedu opfu laa azụ azụ. Ọo-gbalihu tsoo ibe ndu ẹjo-iphe ọgu; mẹkpoo ndu anọduje eyeru ndu eme ẹjo-ememe ẹka. 3 Obenu lẹ ndu Ijiputu bụ nemadzụ; ẹphe ta abụdu Chileke. Ịnya phẹ bẹ bụ anụ-ẹhu; ọ tọ bụdu maa. Chipfu -machịwaa ẹka bẹ ụpfu a-dụ-a onye eye ẹka. Onye eeyeru ẹka adaa gwoo. Ẹphenebo atụko laa l'ite lanụ.
4 Wakwa iphe, Chipfu pfuru nụ mu baa: “Ọo g'oduma asụje ekiri; mbụ g'oke-oduma asụje ekiri mẹ o -gbua iphe, oo-ri; m'obetakpọo e kua ikpoto ndu eche atụru; ẹphe tụko dzukọo g'ẹphe tsoo ya ọgu; mkpu, ẹphe tụko echi te emekpọdanu gẹ ndzụ gụa ya; ọphu ụzu, ẹphe atụ akpọshidu iya iphe, oome-a. Noo gẹ Chipfu, bụ Ọkalibe-Kakọta-Ike e-nyizeta bya alwụa ọgu l'eli úbvú Zayọnu; mẹkpoo lẹ mkpakpọnu úbvú ono. 5 Chipfu, bụ Ọkalibe-Kakọta-Ike e-kpuchita Jierúsalẹmu g'ẹnu, ephe l'eli. Oo-kpuchi iya; tẹme l'ọ nafụtakwa iya phọ; agbaru iya ọdzori, agbaru iya mkpu; g'ọ tọ dụ iphe, e-me iya nụ.”
6 “Sụ-a; unubẹ ndu Ízurẹlu; unu lwaphutanụ azụ lwapfuta onye ono, unu kwefuru ike ono; 7 kẹle o -rwụa mbọku ono bẹ onyemonye lẹ g'unu ha e-mechakwaa jịka nshi mkpọla-ọchaa ono; yẹe kẹ mkpọla-ododo ono, unu gude ẹka iphe-ẹji unu mee ono. 8 Ndu Asiriya bẹ ee-gude ogu-echi, abụdu k'amadụ gude gbushia; mbụ ọ bụru ogu-echi, abụdu kẹ nemadzụ mmanụ bẹ ee-gude gbufu phẹ. Ẹphe a-gba ọso gbalaaru ogu-echi ono; l'a chịru ụnwokorobya phẹ ye l'ozi mkpọnwu-ẹhu. 9 Agbarike phẹ a-dakọta; ẹka eeye phẹ ebvu. Ndu-ishi ndu ojọgu phẹ -hụmaepho g'ọgu ọbu eje ala; ẹphe agbado ẹkwa-ọhubama phẹ phọta ọso gbalachaa.” Noo iphe, Chipfu pfuru bụ onoya; mbụ onye ono, kpọberu ọku l'úbvú Zayọnu; bya azụbe ọta pfụpfupfu ọku lẹ Jierúsalẹmu ono.
