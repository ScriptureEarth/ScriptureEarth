Ebvu-ọma, Dévidi gụru gude nyatarụ Chipfu iphe
38
Gụbe Chipfu; te egudekwa
ẹhu-eghu ngu baarụ
mu mba!
Ọphu i gudekwa oke ẹhu-eghu
ngu hụ̀a mu ahụ̀hù!
2 Kẹle apfụ nkengu guẹkwaru
mu phọ eguru l'ẹhu;
tẹme ẹka ngu nọdu
apyịpyashi mu apyịpyashi.
3 Ẹhu-eghu ngu meru;
ọphu ẹhu mu l'ophu
enwedu ẹka te emedu
mu ẹhuka.
Iphe-ẹji, mu meru ngu
woẹrupho ọkpu mu
gbaghashịa.
4 Kẹle ẹjo-iphe, mu meshiru
ripyabewaru mu.
Ọ nọdu anyịtaberu ẹrwa
k'ọphu mu adụedu ike
evuvu iya.
5 Onwo, aba mu nụ
eshiwaa tụkuruba.
Ọ bụru iphe, kparụ iya nụ
bụ eswe, mu meru.
6 Mu nọwa kpọyoo dẹe ẹka
l'agba gụkota aphụ
mkpụrumkpuru.
7 Okpurukpu kwatakpọo
emeshi mu ẹhuka ike;
mbụ l'ọ tọ dụdu ẹka ẹhu te
emedu mu ẹhuka mẹ nanụ.
8 Ẹhu mu l'ophu dahuwaru
adzụlakpa;
tẹme obu mu tsukọtawa
gworigwori.
Obu te ebvuduru mu
ebvuru k'ọphu bụ lẹ
mu atsụshi ude ike.
9 Gụbe Chipfu; iphe bụkpoo
iphe, agụ mu nụ bẹ ị
makọtaru.
Ude, mu atsụ bẹ e te
emeduru ngu lẹ mpya.
10 Obu etsu mu gbudugbudu;
ọphu ike adụkpoedu
mu ililekpọo.
Mu nọdu aphụwaa ragaraga.
11 Ndu yeru mu obu;
yẹe ndu mu l'ẹphe eshi
ọ̀nyà bufuwaru
mu iche.
Ọ bụru iphe-ẹhuka, dapfutaru
mu nụ kparụ iya.
Ndu mu l'ẹphe bụ abụbu je
apfụchaaru ụzenya.
12 Ndu achọ ụzo, ẹphe e-shi
gbua mu woru ọ́nyà
gbakarụ mu.
Ndu tụberu g'ẹphe meka mu
iphe nọdu anmarụ
mu ọnwu.
Ọo ẹpho idzu g'ẹphe
e-shi ghọoru mu ụgho
bẹ ẹphe anọduje achị
eswe l'ẹnyashi.
13 Mbẹdua dụkwa g'onye ada
nkụchi;
k'ọphu bụ l'ọ tọ dụdu iphe,
mu anụmaje.
Mu dụkwapho g'onye
ete epfudu opfu;
woẹrupho ọnu mu
dobe doo.
14 Mu dụwaa g'onye eepfu opfu
eyeru; ọphu o yedu ọnu;
kẹle ọ tọ dụdu iphe, eepfu,
mu nụmaru.
15 Obenu l'obu mu daru
ngabẹru gụbe Chipfu.
Ọ bụru gụbe Nnajịuphu,
bụ Chileke mu bẹ a-za
mu oku, mu eku ngu.
16 Mu sụru-a: “B'ekwekwa
g'ẹphe teru ẹswa
kpua mu!
Nta-a, ọkpa chiswafuru mu-a
bẹ ẹphe tụkowaru etu
onwophẹ ọphushugbu.”
17 Kẹle mu anga anganga;
tẹme aphụ nọdu ejijeẹ mu
phọ obu tekenteke.
18 Mu e-pfushi ẹjo-iphe,
mu meshiru ngu.
Iphe-ẹji, mu meru erwukwa
mu phọ l'ẹhu.
19 Obenu lẹ ndu ọhogu mu
dụ igwerigwe ebu jajaja;
tẹme ndu mu dụ ashị
l'ẹbe adụ iphe,
mu meru phẹ pakwaaphọ
igwerigwe.
20 Ndu egudeje iphe, dụ ẹji pfụa
ụgwo iphe-ọma, e meru phẹ
bẹ etso mu opfu;
kẹle mu anọduje achọ iphe,
dụ ree.
21 Ta agwọbekwa mu gụbe Chipfu!
Gụbe Chileke nkemu;
ba nọdukwa mu ụzenya!
22 Bya ẹgwegwa bya eyeru
mu ẹka; gụbe Chipfu,
bụ onye ndzọta mu!
