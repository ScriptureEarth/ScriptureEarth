Ebvu-ọma, Asafu deru nụ onye-ishi ebvu; g'e gude olu ebvu, “Ojimbvu Ọgbandzu” gụa ya
80
1-2 Ngabẹnu anyi nchị l'olu;
gụbe onye eche Ízurẹlu
g'onye eche atụru;
bya abụru onye anọduje
erwu Jiósẹfu g'onye
erwu igwe atụru eje
lẹ nri.
Gẹ gụbe onye ono,
sụgaru l'echilabọ chierobu
ono chinaa g'anwụ
l'iphu ndu Ifuremu;
waa ndu Benjiaminu;
yẹe ndu Manásẹ!
Jiko rụkotanu ike ngu bya
adzọta anyi!
3 Dophunu anyi azụ l'ọnodu
anyi jiko Chileke!
G'iphu ngu gbua nwịinwii
l'ẹhu anyi;
k'ọphu aa-dzọta anyi.
4 Gụbe Chipfu bụ Chileke;
bya abụru Ọkalibe-Kakọta-Ike;
?ẹhu e-ghukpọo ngu eghu
ghuberu teke ole;
mbụ kẹ g'ị tị nụmaje epfupfu,
ndibe ngu epfu anụ ngu?
5 Nta-a bẹ i mewaru phẹ;
ẹnya-mini bụru phẹ nri
eriri;
bya emee phẹ;
ẹphe ngụa ẹnya-mini,
jiru ochi njaka-ọnu.
6 I mewaru; ndu obutobu anyi
phẹ nọdu ekweru anyi
une;
tẹme ndu ọhogu anyi nọdu
achị anyi ntụmatu.
7 Dophunụ anyi azụ l'ọnodu
anyi; jiko Chileke,
bụ Ọkalibe-Kakọta-Ike!
G'iphu ngu gbua nwịinwii
l'ẹhu anyi;
k'ọphu aa-dzọta anyi.
8 Ọo ngu shi l'alị Ijiputu gude
akpụru vayịnu wolata;
bya atụko ndu ọhozo
chịfuchaa l'alị phẹ;
bya eworu iya kụa.
9 Ọo ngu gude ẹka ngu
gbuchaa ẹka ị kpọberu
iya egbucha.
Ọ bya agbaa ọgbarabvu,
tụkoru alị ono l'ophu
jedzuru.
10 Ọ jaa àjàjà jachikọta úbvú
l'ophu;
mbụ l'ẹkali iya haẹpho
ephekerephe
ephekerephe g'eze
oshi sida.
11 Ẹpfukala iya jedzuru
oke-ẹnyimu.
Ọ tụko ẹkameka kpofushia
jasụ l'ẹnyimu
Yufurétisu.
12 ?Dẹnu g'ọ nwụru ọ gbẹchia
bụru nta-a bẹ i jeru
eworu mgbabu iya
phọkashia;
k'ọphu onyemonye,
aghata nụ ejeje je awọo
akpụru iya g'ọ dụ iya?
13 Ezi-ọswa shi l'oke ọswa
wụ-pfuta iya bya
atakwoshia ya;
tẹme ụnwu anụ,
i meru dobe l'ẹgbudu bụru
iya bẹ ẹphe gude
azụ onwophẹ.
14 Lwaphutanụ azụ lwapfuta
anyi; jiko Chileke,
bụ Ọkalibe-Kakọta-Ike!
Gbẹnu l'imigwe lefu ẹnya
hụmakpodapho iphe,
anwụ nụ.
Jiko letanụ vayịnu
ngu-a ẹnya;
15 mbụ ọgbarabvu iya ono,
bụ gụbedua gude ẹkutara
ngu yee ya;
mbụ nwa nwoke,
bụ gụbedua gude ẹka ngu
zụa ya zụ-tsee.
16 Ome vayịnu ngu ono bẹ
e gbubushiakwarụ.
E gudeakwa ọku tsupyashịa
ya.
Ọ bụru mba, ị gbanwụberu
iphu baarụ ndibe ngu
meru iphe,
ẹphe watarụ alaa l'iyi.
17 Wonụru ẹka tukobe nwoke ono,
nọ ngu l'ẹkutara ono;
mbụ nwa nwoke ono,
bụ gụbedua gude ẹka ngu zụa
ya zụ-tsee ono.
18 Ọo ya bụ g'anyi aghabuhuru
ngu okotazụ.
Jiko phunwunaa anyi g'ọku;
g'anyi awata eku ẹpha
ngu ọzo.
19 Dophunụ anyi azụ l'ọnodu
anyi;
jiko Chipfu, bụ Chileke;
bya abụru
Ọkalibe-Kakọta-Ike!
G'iphu ngu gbua nwịinwii
l'ẹhu anyi;
k'ọphu aa-dzọta anyi.
