Ebvu, Dévidi deru
35
Gụbe Chipfu; ndu etso mu
opfu bụkwa ngu
e-tsolata phẹ!
Ndu ọphu etso mu ọgu
bụkwapho gụbedua
e-je etsolata phẹ!
2 Gbalihu; chịta ngwọgu;
waa
iphe-mgbobuta-ọgu
ngu;
bya egude gbaaru mu
mkpu!
3 Palia arwa ngu;
gude kwachia ndu achị mu
lẹ njọ ụzo!
Sụnu mu l'ịi-dzọ mu-a!
4 Gẹ ndu achọ ụzo, ẹphe e-shi
gbua mu bụru ndu e
meru iphe-iphere.
G'a chiphukwa ndu achịru
mu ẹjo idzu azụ.
G'a tụ phẹ ẹberebete!
5 G'ẹphe dụ g'ẹswa ereshi,
phẹrephere zikashịru teke
ojozi-imigwe Chipfu
a-nọdu achị phẹ ọso.
6 G'ụzo phẹ gbakọtakwa
ọchii;
chikwaphọ kẹ
bụrukutuku
teke Ojozi-imigwe Chipfu
a-nọdu achị phẹ
ịkpabaka.
7 Eshinu ọ dụdu iphe, mu meru
phẹ; ẹphe je agbabẹru
mu ọ́nyà;
bya eworu nsụ bua gẹ mu
nwulahụ;
8 g'ọla-l'iswi tụkwaa phẹ
l'ụpfu.
G'ọ́nyà, ẹphe gbabẹru
l'onwophẹ nmatakwaa
phẹ.
G'ẹphe nwudakpọe ya phọ kẹ
wurukutukumu.
9 Ọo ya bụ gẹ mu etee ẹswa
l'ime Chipfu.
Ẹhu atsọahaa mu l'ọ dzọtaru
mu.
10 Mu e-gudekpọo obu mu g'ọ
ha sụ:
“?Bụ onye atụru gụbe Chipfu?
Ọo ngu adzọtaje ndu ụkpa
l'ẹka ndu ka phẹ
ọkpehu.
Mbụ l'ịinafutaje ndu ụkpa;
waa ndu adụdu g'ọ dụ
phẹ l'ẹka ndu ana phẹ
nfụ.”
11 Ndu ẹjo-ekebe wulihuru ebo
mu ibo.
Ẹphe chịkoo iphe, mu amadụ
kụbe sụ l'ọo mu bẹ o
shi l'ẹka.
12 Ẹphe gude ẹjo-ememe pfụa
mu ụgwo iphe-ọma.
Ọo ya bụ lẹ mu dụwaa g'onye
a tọgboru nwa maa.
13 Obenu lẹ teke iphe-ememe shi
zụu phẹ bẹ mu wotaru
uwe-aphụ yee;
bya ewoze onwomu alị aswị
ẹgu epfu anụ Chipfu;
ọle Chipfu ta anụmaduru
olu mu.
14 Mu nọdu aghaphe egbu
onwomu egbugbu
l'ẹkwa g'ọ bụ ọ̀nyà mu;
ọzoo nwune mu bẹ
iphe-ememe zụpyaberu.
Mu nọdukpoo dẹe ẹka l'agba
g'onye akwa ẹkwa ne iya,
nwụhuru nụ.
15 Obenu l'opfu dụru mbẹdua;
ẹphe gude ẹhu-ụtso
dzukọbe.
Ẹphe swiphee mu
mgburugburu tsua
mu iphe teke mu
akwabẹduru ẹnya;
bya egude njọ-opfu
alabushi anụ mu nanụ
nanụ.
16 Ndu gudewaa echi ndu ọzo
eswe merwushia
onwophẹ l'iphu Chipfu;
nọdu atagbaru mu ikireze
kẹ nyịkinyiki.
17 Gụbe Chipfu; ?bụ teke ole bẹ
ii-jipyaberu onwongu
elekpọepho anyi ẹnya?
Dzọnaa mu; a -nọnya ẹphe
mee mu; mu bụru
ọla-l'iswi!
Dzọtaro mkpakọ iphe ono,
mu nweru ono, bụ ndzụ
mu g'oduma ta
adzụgbu iya!
18 Ọ bụru l'ẹka ọha l'ophu
dzukọberu bẹ mu
a-nọdu kele ngu ekele;
mbụ lẹ mu a-nọdu l'iphu
ikpoto nemadzụ jaa
ngu ajaja.
19 B'ekwekwa gẹ ndu ọhogu mu
teru ẹswa kpua mu;
mbụ ndu kpọru mu ashị;
ẹbe adụ iphe, mu
meru phẹ.
Ọphu i kwekwa gẹ ndu ono,
mu dụ ashị ono bvua mu
ẹnya ria.
20 Kẹle ẹphe te epfujedu opfu
nchị-adụ-doo;
obenu l'iphe, abụjeru ozi,
dụru phẹ nụ bụ achị idzu
g'ẹphe e-shi ghọoru ndu dụ
àgù lẹ mkpụkpu ụgho.
21 Ẹphe sarụ ọnu galaba wata
mu ebo ibo sụ:
“Ee! Anyi gudekpọo ẹnya
anyi hụma iphe,
i meru!”
22 Ọle gụbe Chipfu hụmanuru-a
iphemiphe ono.
Jiko ba agbakwa mu nkụchi
ọphu ị nọdukwa
mu ụzenya!
23 Tehu etehu; kwolihurọ bya
ahaaru mu enge!
Gbaaru mu ọdzori;
jiko Nnajịuphu mu,
bụ Chileke mu!
24 Gude apfụbekoto nkengu
haarụ mu rọ enge; jiko
Chipfu, bụ Chileke mu!
Te kwekwa g'ẹphe teru ẹswa
kpua mu!
25 Te kwekwa g'ẹphe rịa l'obu
phẹ sụ:
“Nta bẹ anyi chọ-vuwaru iphe,
anyi achọ!”
Ọzoo g'ẹphe sụ: “Nta-a bẹ
anyi patawaru
iya lwee!”
26 G'iphe, bụkpoo ndu ete ẹswa
ekpu ẹjo-iphe, byaru
mu nụ bụkwaru ndu ee-meru
iphere kpua bya atụ
phẹ ẹberebete.
G'e gudekwa iphere dejia phẹ
ẹpho; mbụ ndu ono,
etu onwophẹ
ọphushugbu eje abyapfuta
mu ono!
27 Gẹ ndu ele ẹnya g'a haarụ
mu enge gụa ebvu ẹhu-ụtso
tee ẹswa!
G'iphe, ẹphe a-kpụjeru l'ọnu
bụekwarupho: “G'a tụa
ọnu Chipfu l'ọ parụ ẹka;
mbụ onye ọoduje ree mẹ
onye-ozi iya nọdu buru
l'ẹhu-guu.”
28 Ọo ya bụ; mu egude ọnu
mu pfua
k'apfụbekoto nkengu.
Ọ bụkwarupho gẹ mu
a-nọdu etu ngu ẹpha
mbọku-mbọku
bụ onoya.
